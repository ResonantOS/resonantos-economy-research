## Executive Finding

The harvest supports a set of bridge-labeled research controls, not an economy
mechanism. The strongest local result is a boundary discipline: observed
signals, reviewed credit, rewards, and governance authority must remain
separate until a named owner route, workflow witness, adversarial failure check,
and promotion gate exist. The reviewer pass demoted every attractive mechanism
phrase into candidate-control language, with open residue preserved where
behavioral, legal, governance, privacy, or anti-gaming evidence is missing.

## Blocked Claims

- ResonantOS economy mechanisms are solved, validated, proven, or ready for
  implementation.
- Any token, payout, bounty, DAO distribution, reward adapter, or financial
  launch path is ready.
- Reputation, credit, citations, audit hits, prices, token balances, or
  reviewer attention grant governance authority by themselves.
- `ObservedUseReceipt` is a promoted schema or proof of contribution.
- Credit conservation, anti-gaming, collusion resistance, or bounty-farming
  resistance is solved.
- Public lineage export is safe without a privacy and redaction policy.
- DomainSpec Lean proves ResonantOS economic fairness, reward safety,
  randomness safety, or launch readiness.
- A clean audit, build status, prize label, inventory entry, or dispatch
  consensus can certify a whole economy mechanism.
- External DAO, science-credit, behavioral, or mechanism-design sources locally
  support claims before source records, bridge decisions, and local relevance
  checks exist.

## Reviewer Attack Summary

The reviewer group did not approve any selector as a mechanism. Its useful
contribution was demotion and boundary sharpening:

- `economy-credit-before-money-witness` was demoted to
  `credit-before-money-sequencing-hypothesis`.
- `host-observed-reuse-receipt` was narrowed to
  `one-observed-surface-receipt-candidate`.
- `signal-credit-authority-separation` was split into
  `observed-signal-not-credit`, `credit-record-not-reward`,
  `attention-route-not-authority`, and `owner-route-authority-only`.
- `conserved-credit-anti-gaming` was killed as a strong name and salvaged as
  `credit-gaming-failure-fixtures`.
- `contribution-classes-not-entitlements` was demoted to
  `candidate-contribution-taxonomy-not-entitlement`.
- `reward-adapter-phase2-gate` was renamed
  `phase2-financial-blocker-register`; it is a blocker register, not an unlock
  gate.
- `public-private-lineage-boundary` survived only as
  `privacy-redaction-policy-prerequisite`.
- `invariant-to-gate-chain-with-witness` became
  `invariant-drafting-route-open-witness-or-typed-negative`.
- `claim-status-and-bounded-safety-language`,
  `two-layer-independent-audits`, and `cite-demote-bridge-labels` survived only
  as field discipline, multi-axis audit discipline, and source-card intake
  discipline.

The systems warning from the reviewer pass is that attention routing, credit,
review, and recognition can themselves become pressure surfaces. Any later
mechanism work must therefore test delayed feedback, capture, bottlenecks,
incumbency, collusion, and governance loops separately instead of collapsing
them into a single score or approval result.
