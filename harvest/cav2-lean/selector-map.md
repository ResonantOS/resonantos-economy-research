## Reviewer Revision Rule

The reviewer group rejected several strong selector names as overclaiming. The
current map therefore uses candidate-control language:

- `witness` became `hypothesis`, `candidate`, or `next check` unless an actual
  workflow witness is named.
- `reward adapter` became `financial blocker register`.
- `conserved credit` became `credit-gaming failure fixtures`.
- broad `signal-credit-authority separation` became four separate checks.
- bridge controls survive only with source kind, target file, next check, and
  blocked-use discipline.

## Blocked Claims

Blocked until separate authority gates and witnesses exist:

- ResonantOS economy mechanism is solved or validated.
- Token, payout, bounty, DAO distribution, reward adapter, or financial launch
  is ready.
- Reputation, credit, citations, audit hits, prices, or token balances grant
  governance authority.
- `ObservedUseReceipt` is a promoted schema.
- Credit conservation or anti-gaming is solved.
- Public lineage export is safe without a privacy/redaction policy.
- Lean formalization proves ResonantOS economic fairness, reward safety, or
  randomness safety.
- Attention routing cannot become de facto authority.
- External DAO, science-credit, behavioral, or mechanism-design evidence locally
  supports claims before source records and bridge decisions exist.
