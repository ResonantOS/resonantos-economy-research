## One Sentence

A durable ResonantOS economy should begin with authority-separated credit and reviewed contribution witnesses, not with bounties, score-governance, or financial-token mechanisms.

## Operator Model

```text
Do not ask first: how do we pay?
Ask first: what proof makes a contribution visible without letting the proof become authority?
Then ask: how can actors game it, how does it change behavior, and what gates stop it from governing too early?
```

## What To Borrow Carefully

- `$RCT` as non-transferable contribution reputation.
- `$RES` as internal utility first, with liquidity delayed.
- Credit-before-money from Evidence Exchange.
- DomainSpec's separation of mechanism correctness, temporal accumulation, and self-description.

## What To Keep Analogy-Only

- Academic citation systems.
- Commons governance systems.
- Market design and mechanism design papers.
- DAO postmortems.

## What To Block

- Bounty-first economy design.
- Score-to-authority promotion.
- Token-price or liquidity as evidence of community health.
- AI-agent human governance rights.
- Phase 2 design without failure precedent and evidence harness.
