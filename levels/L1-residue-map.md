## Three-View Frame

| View | Question | Failure if ignored |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Socioeconomic mentalities and behavior | What kind of person, habit, trust relation, and community posture does the mechanism train? | A community becomes a task-harvesting labor market. |
| Algorithmic reward and randomness | What rules, uncertainty, scoring, and constraints make gaming expensive? | The mechanism becomes a predictable extraction target. |
| Authority and formal invariants | Which signals can matter without deciding authority, governance, or launch readiness? | Scores become law without a gate. |

## DomainSpec Formalization Borrow

The formalization side contributes a warning, not an economy theorem:

- schema-level correctness and instance-level behavior need separate checks;
- temporal accumulation does not self-cancel;
- self-describing systems need explicit stopping criteria because more review layers do not close the loop by themselves.

Economy reading: a reward design needs separate audits for mechanism shape, behavior over time, and governance self-reference.

## Harvested Control Residue

The CAv2/Lean harvest did not close any mechanism. It converted local source
material into candidate controls:

- `observed-signal-not-credit`
- `credit-record-not-reward`
- `attention-route-not-authority`
- `owner-route-authority-only`
- `credit-gaming-failure-fixtures`
- `phase2-financial-blocker-register`
- `privacy-redaction-policy-prerequisite`

These controls are residue handles until concrete workflow witnesses,
behavioral evidence, external bridge sources, and authority gates are present.
