# L0 Corpus

Status: active
Promotion scope: local-research-only

## Corpus Rule

The official ResonantDAO tower has exactly one primary source: `https://resonantdao.com/whitepaper/`.

All other materials are supporting or related sources. They may challenge, contextualize, or propose mechanisms, but they do not redefine what ResonantDAO says about itself.

## Existing Local Thesis To Reuse Carefully

The Evidence Exchange proposal already gives this tower a useful non-collapse baseline:

- credit can be reviewed and useful without becoming authority;
- tokens and payouts are future adapters, not the foundation;
- authority movement requires owner routes;
- lineage and receipts should be useful before money exists.

This tower should reuse those ideas as local design pressure, not as proof that the ResonantDAO economy is solved.
