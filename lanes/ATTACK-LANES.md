# Attack Lanes

Status: active
Promotion scope: local-research-only

## Recommended Order

### Lane 1: Authority-Separated Credit

Attack first.

```text
one observed host surface -> receipt candidate -> reviewed credit record -> eligibility or attention signal
authority_effect = none
governance_power_effect = none until owner gate
payout_effect = none until Phase 2 financial blocker register is cleared by authority route
attention_capture_check = required
```

Why first: it is closest to existing CyberAlchemy Evidence Exchange work and
safest against premature token design, but the CAv2/Lean harvest demotes it to
a hypothesis and receipt candidate. It is not a validated witness yet.

### Lane 2: Reputation Capture And `$RCT`

Attack second.

Question: if `$RCT` cannot be transferred, how can it still be farmed, colluded over, socially captured, or converted into governance influence too early?

Expected outputs:

- contribution categories that should not count yet;
- decay, review, challenge, and maintenance questions;
- thresholds that require human or owner review.

### Lane 3: `$RES` Utility And Emission Risk

Attack third.

Question: how can a transferable Phase 1 utility/reward token stay useful without becoming the main game?

Expected outputs:

- utility-only constraints;
- liquidity delay gates;
- treasury spend boundaries;
- anti-sybil and anti-farming checks.

### Lane 4: Bounty And Prize Design

Attack fourth, only after Lanes 1-3.

Question: what difference should exist between a bounty, a prize, a review sponsorship, a maintenance reward, and a contribution-income pathway?

Expected outputs:

- blocked bounty types;
- allowed bounded tasks;
- prize-like open exploration model;
- maintenance-first incentives.

### Lane 5: Phase 2 Financial Tokens

Do not attack as implementation. Treat as research and failure-precedent only.

Question: what evidence would be required before `$R10`, `$R12`, `$R15`, UCI payout, buyback/burn, liquidity management, or reserve mechanisms become designable?

Expected outputs:

- risk threshold matrix;
- stress test categories;
- launch blocker register;
- legal/security/treasury handoff residue.

## Program-Extension Evidence Perspective

The [monetary-circulation tower](../subtowers/monetary-circulation/README.md)
feeds Lane 3 and Lane 5 with one source-first view across supply state, release
schedules, distribution, treasury resilience, liquidity and exit capacity,
utility and sinks, and feedback. It is a research perspective, not a sixth
mechanism lane: its job is to connect evidence that these two attack lanes need
without turning that connection into token, treasury, market, or launch policy.
Its bounded dispatch closed with auditor ACCEPT: generic feedback as one
experiment was killed, a top-level health verdict is prohibited, and every
surviving local handoff remains `unrunnable`.
