# Authority Model

Status: active local authority model
Owner: ResonantOS economy research owner

## Purpose

This model decides what this standalone repository is allowed to claim while it
researches a resilient ResonantOS / ResonantDAO economy.

The model deliberately separates:

- official ResonantDAO source claims;
- repository-local method authority (its research-credibility constitution);
- formalization discipline (invariant-writing precedent, kept as supporting source);
- external research evidence;
- local synthesis;
- policy or implementation authority, which this repo does not own.

## Core Rule

Research can propose mechanisms, invariants, workflows, and gates. Research
cannot promote them into economy policy, token design, governance weights, payout
rules, or implementation without an external owner decision.

## Authority Kinds

| Kind | Counts as | Owner route | Blocked use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Primary source | What ResonantDAO says about itself. | Official-source reading route. | Treating local synthesis as official position. |
| Method authority | Research process, source posture, promotion discipline, and non-collapse rules. | Repository-local research-credibility constitution. | Treating method authority as mechanism validation. |
| Formalization discipline | Invariant-writing and proof-honesty posture. | Repository-local formalization precedent (harvested, kept as supporting source). | Claiming Lean proof of economics without a Lean artifact. |
| Related evidence | DAO, crypto, science-of-science, mechanism-design, and behavioral sources. | Relevant subtower. | Treating analogy as proof. |
| Local synthesis | A repo-authored reading or hypothesis. | Tower or lane owner review. | Becoming policy by proximity. |
| Candidate invariant | A proposed condition that should remain true. | Closure plan plus validation route. | Becoming a rule before witness and gate. |
| Workflow witness | A concrete scenario that can test a candidate mechanism. | Evidence harness or validation route. | Being treated as general proof from one example. |
| External policy authority | Token, governance, bounty, payout, launch, or product decision. | ResonantDAO / ResonantOS owner route. | This repo cannot exercise it directly. |

## Source Movement

```text
source evidence
  -> source record
  -> source claim ledger
  -> subtower synthesis
  -> candidate invariant or workflow witness
  -> validation request
  -> external owner decision
```

No arrow is automatic. A candidate can be blocked, demoted, split, or sent back
to corpus work.

## Non-Collapse Rules

1. Contribution credit is not governance authority.
2. Reputation is not proof of truth.
3. Token transferability is not community value.
4. Bounty completion is not long-term contribution by itself.
5. Randomness is not fairness unless the fairness target and audit path are
   explicit.
6. A mechanism that is incentive-compatible in a model is not socially safe
   until behavioral residue is named.
7. A source from failed DAO history is warning evidence, not destiny.
8. Harvested external method precedent supports research credibility, not final
   economy correctness.

## Gate Vocabulary

- `pass`: evidence, source kind, owner route, and validation surface are adequate
  for the next research move.
- `flag`: work may continue with named risk and follow-up.
- `block`: stop until a source, authority, witness, or boundary gap closes.
- `defer`: not rejected, but the route or timing is not ready.
