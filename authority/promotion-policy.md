# Promotion Policy

Status: active local policy
Owner: ResonantOS economy research owner

Research promotes by evidence and owner route, not by elegance.

## Promotion Ladder

| State | Meaning | May promote to |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Source evidence | Original source material in its own context. | Source record. |
| Source record | Path, date, source kind, and boundary captured. | Source claim ledger. |
| Source claim | A claim with evidence and local reading separated. | Subtower synthesis. |
| Subtower synthesis | Bounded domain reading with source-kind labels. | Candidate invariant, workflow witness, or open residue. |
| Candidate invariant | A proposed condition the economy should preserve. | Validation request after collapse test and witness. |
| Workflow witness | A concrete scenario for testing mechanism behavior. | Evidence harness or external review. |
| Research recommendation | A source-backed proposal with risks and gates. | External owner decision. |

## Required Gates

| Gate | Required when | Standard |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Source-kind gate | A new source is added or cited. | Primary, supporting, related, analogy, inference, or residue is explicit. |
| Authority gate | A claim could affect governance, token, bounty, payout, or launch decisions. | Route to `authority/` and mark non-policy until external owner review. |
| Failure-corpus gate | A bounty, token, treasury, DAO, or market mechanism is proposed. | Known failure modes and exploit incentives are named first. |
| Behavioral gate | A mechanism changes rewards, status, reputation, or visibility. | Motivation, trust, identity, status, and gaming effects are considered. |
| Formal gate | A mechanism claims invariance, incentive compatibility, robustness, or randomness benefits. | Model assumptions, collapse test, and witness path are named. |
| Phase-2 gate | A financial mechanism is discussed. | It remains future-work unless readiness and risk controls are externally approved. |

## Promotion Blocks

Block promotion when:

- the mechanism rewards task-harvesting more than durable contribution;
- the metric becomes governance authority without human/contextual review;
- randomness hides discretion or produces arbitrary-feeling rewards;
- the claim depends on an analogy without bridge decision;
- the proposal skips failed-DAO or exploit precedent;
- the recommendation has no witness, falsifier, or audit route.
