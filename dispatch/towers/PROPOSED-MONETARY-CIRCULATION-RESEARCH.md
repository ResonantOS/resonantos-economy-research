## Program Extension Posture

The completed four-tower meta-program and its registered child dispatches are
historical frozen evidence. This proposal does not edit or retroactively expand
those confirmed sheets. It proposes a fifth source-corpus perspective that will
tag findings into existing attack Lane 3 (`$RES` utility and emission) and Lane
5 (Phase 2 financial mechanisms).

The existing A/B/C labels describe the current fixture, authority, and
constitution backlog. They are not research corpora. A later Track D may hold
surviving monetary-resilience fixtures, but only after this source synthesis;
the present proposal does not create or validate that fixture track.

## P1 Trigger

Dispatch is warranted by all four Principle-1 triggers:

- **synthesis:** four distinct external corpora must be reconciled with existing
  Lane 3, Lane 5, TF-1, TF-12, TF-13, TF-14, and ER-D24 through ER-D27;
- **context protection:** market, treasury, issuance, and behavioral source
  returns would be substantially larger than the parent synthesis needs;
- **isolation:** each source cohort must remain discardable until its source
  posture and Resonant bridge survive review; and
- **parallelism:** the four external cohorts are independent before synthesis.

This is a bounded source-first run, not a token-policy, treasury-policy,
liquidity-deployment, or financial-launch exercise.

## Goal

Build a source-backed research map of supply state, release schedules,
distribution, treasury resilience, liquidity and exit capacity, utility and
sinks, and monetary feedback, then determine which candidate experiment
handoffs are owned, witnessed, definitionally sound, blocked, or still residue.

## Existing Decisions That Must Not Be Reversed Silently

- The official ResonantDAO whitepaper remains the only official primary source.
- Existing Lane 3 and Lane 5 Resonant-specific financial mappings remain analogy
  or future work where the synthesis demoted them.
- ER-D24 Treasury, ER-D25 Epoch, ER-D26 Emission, and ER-D27 Runway remain
  candidate definitions with named grounding gaps.
- The flow-conservation file remains a structure-only skeleton, not a fixture or
  validated invariant.
- Numeric treasury, liquidity, drawdown, depeg, slippage, time-to-cash,
  concentration, or launch thresholds are parameters until sourced and
  calibrated.
- A monetary metric cannot grant contribution credit, reward, reputation,
  governance weight, or authority.
