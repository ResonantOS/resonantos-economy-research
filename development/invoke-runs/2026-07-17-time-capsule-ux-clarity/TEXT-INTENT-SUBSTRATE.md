# Whisper Text Intent Substrate

Status: `pass`

Transport: `interactive-web-copy-extension` (`candidate` / unproven)

## Resonance Core

### Living center

Many small human traces become one shared beginning, then wait to meet the
community again. The text should feel like an invitation to leave something
small inside a long future—not an administrative process and not a token sale.

### Emotional temperature

Warm, curious, grounded, patient, quietly consequential.

### Voice behaviors

- collective “we” where it creates shared ownership;
- direct questions where they open imagination;
- short contrasts where they remove confusion;
- concrete verbs: leave, close, shape, choose, return;
- permission to be quiet;
- one image carried through: a capsule that chooses one beginning and returns
  three times.

### Forbidden drift

- bureaucratic governance language;
- mystical language without a concrete meaning;
- financial excitement, winning, or reward framing;
- loyalty pressure or compulsory togetherness;
- claims of privacy, fairness, permanence, or launch readiness;
- treating a word as a token score.

## Relevance Core

### Audience

ResonantDAO community members and founding contributors arriving with little
context. Some will be interested in the idea but not the mechanism. Some will
be wary of token claims or forced participation. Some may only give the page a
few seconds.

### What must land quickly

1. This is a time capsule.
2. A person may leave up to two words for each 2, 5, and 10-year return.
3. All words together help select the initial number of tokens once.
4. The words return later; the token number does not change.
5. Participation brings no personal tokens, status, or penalty.
6. The current experience is a browser-only rehearsal.

### Reader objections

- “Is this a lottery I can win?”
- “Does each word equal some token amount?”
- “Do I have to submit six words?”
- “Why do the words come back if the number is already chosen?”
- “Are my saved words actually private?”
- “Did I just submit something?”

## Trajectory Core

```text
inviting question
  -> name the time capsule
  -> show the small voluntary action
  -> show many words becoming one shared pattern
  -> separate the one-time number from the three future returns
  -> rehearse safely
  -> close without false submission
```

## Proof And Boundary Core

- Mechanism copy distinguishes the complete capsule pattern from a letter or
  word score.
- “Helps choose” preserves the later public input.
- “Number of tokens ResonantDAO begins with” names the consequence once, then
  “starting number” can carry it.
- “Saved for later” stays unavailable in the preview and makes no protection
  claim.
- The public manifesto influences voice only; approved local specifications own
  all product claims.
