## Required Corrections

- Do not use “lottery” without first stating that nobody wins or receives a
  personal allocation. Prefer omitting it from the core journey.
- Do not shorten “shared digital pattern” to “your number”; the result belongs
  to the whole capsule.
- Do not use “private word” for saved-for-later intent.
- Do not say future openings “reveal the number”; the number is selected once
  after the capsule closes.
- Do not use “submission” on the rehearsal receipt.

## Consistency Result

The copy deck preserves the approved product distinctions while substituting
plain-language aliases at first contact. The only flagged alias is “saved for
later,” whose feature remains visibly unavailable and unproven.
