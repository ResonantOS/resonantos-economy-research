## Voice Validation

The rewrite uses collective language and crisp contrasts while keeping product
mechanics concrete. It does not copy manifesto sentences. The voice reference
influences cadence and stance only.

but could still imply pressure in a founding context. The immediate “if you
want,” optional labels, and quiet path balance it. Human testing must verify
that this is felt, not merely intended.
