# Whisper Learning Residue

## Preserved Lessons

1. **Name the container before its mechanism.** “Time capsule” gives the reader
   a known object; “Number Lottery” creates an unrelated contest model.
2. **Carry one image.** One beginning and three returns is more memorable than
   four equal rule cards.
3. **Separate the clocks.** The number happens once; the words return later.
   This correction deserves its own sentence.
4. **Explain the whole before the proof.** “One shared pattern” makes the idea
   legible; digital fingerprint and public-input details can follow.
5. **Optionality must be operable.** Saying “optional” is weaker than providing
   an all-empty path named “Continue quietly.”
6. **Safety belongs beside its consequence.** Public persistence belongs by the
   visibility choice; rehearsal truth belongs at entry, confirmation, and
   receipt.
7. **Impact does not require grandeur.** A direct future-facing question plus a
   small action carries more meaning than abstract language about ceremony.

## Open Questions For Testing

- Does “our future” welcome non-founding members or feel ownership-exclusive?
- Does “shape one beginning” clearly imply influence without implying control?
- Is “one shared pattern” concrete enough before the disclosure is opened?
- Do people interpret “visible with the capsule” as public?
- Does showing three horizons at once still make the form feel like six tasks?
