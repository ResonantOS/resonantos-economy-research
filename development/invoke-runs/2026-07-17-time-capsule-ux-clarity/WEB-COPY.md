## Mechanism Disclosure

**Collapsed label**

How do words reach the number?

**Expanded heading**

The whole capsule matters—not a letter score.

### 1. We close the capsule

When the contribution window ends, the accepted words become one final record.
No one can add or change a word after seeing the result.

### 2. The record becomes one shared pattern

Every accepted word changes the pattern of the capsule as a whole. A word is
never worth a fixed number of tokens by itself.

### 3. A later public number joins it

The public number arrives after the capsule is closed, so it could not be known
when people chose their words.

### 4. The result enters the range chosen before we began

The community agrees on the possible range first. The final result becomes the
candidate number of tokens ResonantDAO begins with.

**Timing correction**

This happens once. The 2, 5, and 10-year openings bring back words; they do not
choose a new token number.
