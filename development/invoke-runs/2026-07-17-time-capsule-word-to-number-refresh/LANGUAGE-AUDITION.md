# Whisper Language Audition

Transport: interactive web copy extension

Status: `candidate`

## Moment 1 — Opening

**Audience-facing copy**

> **How does a word reach the number?**
>
> A word does not receive a token value. It becomes one record in the capsule.
> When the capsule closes, all its records become one digital fingerprint.
> That fingerprint and a new public value then select one number inside the
> range chosen before anyone contributed.

**Audition result**

- exact reader question: pass;
- causal order: pass;
- no unearned jargon: flag — “digital fingerprint” needs the pipeline beside it;
- reward boundary: pass.

## Moment 2 — Tension

**Audience-facing copy**

> **We do not count letters or add word scores.**
>
> `resonance` becomes a record such as “5 years · first word · visible.” That
> record joins every other final record. Change any record and the fingerprint
> of the whole capsule changes.

**Audition result**

- answers arithmetic assumption: pass;
- uses a concrete record: pass;
- whole-capsule effect: pass in author review, human gate pending.

## Moment 3 — Reveal

**Audience-facing copy**

> The capsule is locked before the new public value exists. Until that value
> arrives, there is no final number to reveal. Once it arrives, the same public
> rule turns the fingerprint and the new value into one number inside the range
> agreed at the beginning.

**Audition result**

- explains later-value purpose: pass in author review;
- preserves frozen range: pass;
- avoids claiming a selected provider: pass;
- comprehension proof: pending.

## Audition Verdict

The language is ready for a local implementation candidate. It is not approved
as understood copy until first-time readers retell the full chain.
