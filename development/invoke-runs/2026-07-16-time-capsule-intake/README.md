## Outcome

This run replaces the old “one person, one word” explanation with a complete
three-horizon contribution model:

- each admitted participant may leave zero, one, or two words for the 2-year
  opening;
- zero, one, or two words for the 5-year opening;
- zero, one, or two words for the 10-year opening;
- each filled slot is either `public-now` or `sealed-until-release`;
- all accepted slots enter one founding capsule and shape one initial supply;
- the 2-, 5-, and 10-year openings never recalculate that supply.

The design also prepares the intake form, including public copy, states,
validation rules, data contracts, and a non-collecting HTML wireframe.
