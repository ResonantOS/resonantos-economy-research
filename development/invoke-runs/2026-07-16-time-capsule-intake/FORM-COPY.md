# Form And Public Explanation Copy

Tone: direct, warm, and invitational

## Short Explanation For The First Page

### A time capsule with three openings

You have two places at each opening: two years, five years, and ten years. Leave
none, one, or two words in each. A word can be visible from the beginning or
saved for that future opening.

When the contribution period ends, every accepted place becomes one locked
record. Together, those records become one digital fingerprint for the whole
capsule. We mix that fingerprint with a public value that arrives after the
capsule is locked, then place the result inside the number range chosen in
advance. That gives ResonantDAO its starting supply.

Your word is never a fixed amount of tokens. It changes the shared fingerprint
by taking one place in the capsule. The number is made once; the words return at
2, 5, and 10 years without changing it.

## Visibility Choices

### Visible now

The community can see this word from the beginning and revisit it at this opening.

### Save for this opening

The word stays out of public view now. At this opening, we ask again before
showing it. No answer keeps it sealed.

Prototype note: saving real words is disabled until the protection, recovery,
and stewardship plan has been tested.
