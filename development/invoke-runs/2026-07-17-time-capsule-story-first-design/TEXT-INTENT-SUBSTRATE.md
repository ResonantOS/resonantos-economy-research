# Text Intent Substrate

## Intent

Invite a person who knows nothing about the project to leave a message or any small mark
from the present—for their future self, for someone else, or for the community to meet in
2, 5, or 10 years—or to leave nothing at all. Let them understand how what is left joins a
shared capsule that helps choose how many tokens ResonantDAO starts with.

## Audience

ResonantDAO community and founding contributors, especially first-time visitors with no
cryptography, token, or governance context.

## Voice

Warm, direct, curious, personal without becoming private-by-default, and grounded. Short spoken phrases. No manifesto mimicry,
legalistic warnings, or ceremonial commands.

## Meaning Cores

| SCU | Core meaning | Must survive |
| --- | --- | --- |
| SCU-01 | This is a time capsule | present-to-future relationship |
| SCU-02 | I may leave a message, any small mark, or nothing at all | voluntary agency visible from the opening |
| SCU-02A | The message may be for me, another person, or the wider community | open direction |
| SCU-03 | What I leave becomes one record in a shared whole; an empty place creates no invented record | no personal score |
| SCU-04 | The shared fingerprint and closing block help choose the starting token number | causal clarity |
| SCU-05 | The starting token number is chosen once; messages return later | timing clarity |
| SCU-06 | Public and saved-for-opening are different | reveal choice |
| SCU-07 | This page is only a rehearsal | no false submission |
| SCU-08 | Each opening has a different reason to return | 2 years recognizes continuity; 5 years reveals change; 10 years carries memory |

## Forbidden Drift

- “Your phrase becomes X tokens.”
- “Bitcoin stores the messages.”
- “Saved” means the current prototype protects or recovers anything.
- Participation grants allocation, status, influence, or reward.
- Every horizon must be filled.
- Later openings choose new numbers.
- Every message must address the community.
- Poetic time prompts make the visitor infer whether the field is for 2, 5, or 10 years.
- Repeating “years from now” where a distinct emotional purpose should appear.
