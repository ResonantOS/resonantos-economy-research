# Composition Plan

## Narrative Thesis

Something personal can enter a shared whole without becoming a personal score. Many
small contributions create one capsule; the capsule helps produce one starting number;
the contributions keep their future meaning after the number is chosen.

## Model Change

```text
Before:
  time capsule -> messages open later

After:
  what people leave -> one shared capsule -> one fingerprint + closing signal
                   -> how many tokens ResonantDAO starts with
                   -> messages return in 2 / 5 / 10 years
```

## Narrative Instrument

A folded message parcel of light is the persistent object. It begins alone, meets other
parcels, becomes part of the capsule, contributes to the shared fingerprint, then travels
out again on one of three future paths.

The parcel is not a data packet, token, wallet, or reward. It is a visual witness for a
message or small mark moving from one person into a shared record and later returning.

## Visual Grammar

### Persistent Assets

- `ASSET-FIELD-01`: deep midnight field with fine orbital geometry and restrained amber,
  cream, indigo, and muted cyan light.
- `ASSET-PARCEL-01`: small folded message parcel with a warm luminous seam and no text.
- `ASSET-THREAD-01`: thin light path that belongs to a parcel and joins other paths.
- `ASSET-CAPSULE-01`: one radiant circular capsule, center-right in 16:9 and centered in
  the shared 9:16 crop-safe region.
- `ASSET-FINGERPRINT-01`: concentric geometric trace created by the closed capsule.
- `ASSET-CLOSING-PULSE-01`: one external amber pulse representing the public closing signal.
- `ASSET-TIME-RINGS-01`: three continuing paths and destination rings for 2, 5, and 10 years.

### Camera Language

- Intimate macro framing gives the first parcel personal meaning.
- A widening camera reveals other people and paths without using a globe infographic.
- A controlled push toward the capsule builds collective momentum.
- Stillness at `42` creates impact.
- A pullback after the number reveals the messages continuing into the future.

### Sound Arc

- Begin with room tone and one paper-like fold.
- Give each arriving parcel a quiet tonal identity.
- Let the paths create a low harmonic weave, not a technological data sound.
- Drop almost to silence immediately before `42`.
- Resolve with three distant tones for 2, 5, and 10 years.

## Site Role

The video belongs immediately after the hero or as a play action inside it. The existing
hero image is the poster and visual continuity reference. Playback should be user-started,
captioned, and accompanied by a transcript. The video does not replace the plain-language
message → capsule → number explanation already on the page.
