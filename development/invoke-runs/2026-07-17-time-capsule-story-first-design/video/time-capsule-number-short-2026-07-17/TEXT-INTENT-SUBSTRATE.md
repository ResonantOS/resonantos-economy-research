# Short Video Text Intent Substrate

## Intent

Make a first-time viewer feel that leaving something for the future is personal, shared,
and consequential. In one minute, show that what people leave joins one capsule, the
capsule helps choose how many tokens ResonantDAO starts with, and the messages continue
toward 2, 5, and 10-year returns.

## Audience

People encountering the initiative on the site or through a shared video, without assumed
knowledge of ResonantDAO, token mechanisms, cryptography, or the time-capsule design.

## Resonance Core

- Felt meaning: one small human trace can meet others without losing its future meaning.
- Tone: intimate, warm, quietly cinematic, clear.
- Desired residue: curiosity, belonging, and a wish to leave something for later.
- Forbidden feels: crypto promotion, techno-mysticism, institutional ceremony, pressure,
  surveillance, or wealth imagery.

## Relevance Core

- The viewer first needs a personal reason to care.
- The collective idea becomes visible through separate parcels and paths meeting.
- The number must be named as the starting token number, not an abstract ritual number.
- `42` is memorable only if the film prevents it from posing as the real result.
- Muted social viewing still needs a coherent story through editorial copy.

## Trajectory Core

```text
not for today
  -> for self / another / everyone / or open space
  -> message parcels cross distance
  -> paths connect inside one capsule
  -> fingerprint + closing signal
  -> starting token number
  -> 42 appears visually; the voice says the real one comes later
  -> number stays; messages continue
  -> leave something for later
```

## Meaning Cores

| SCU | Meaning | Must survive |
| --- | --- | --- |
| V-SCU-01 | A message may be personal, relational, or collective | open addressee |
| V-SCU-02 | Leaving something is optional | quiet path without invented record |
| V-SCU-03 | Different records join one shared capsule | collective connection |
| V-SCU-04 | The capsule becomes one fingerprint and meets a closing signal | causal bridge |
| V-SCU-05 | The result is how many tokens ResonantDAO starts with | outcome clarity |
| V-SCU-06 | `42` is visual-only; the voice says the real number comes later | honesty in the impact beat |
| V-SCU-07 | The number happens once; messages return later | time clarity |

## Recomposition Proof

If the package is reduced to these seven cores, all eight scenes can be reconstructed in
their current order. Removing V-SCU-05 produces a generic time-capsule film; removing
V-SCU-06 makes the most memorable frame misleading; removing V-SCU-07 makes the number
and future openings look like the same event.
