# Voice Model Input

Delivery: intimate, warm, quietly cinematic, clear, conversational
Pace: approximately 130 words per minute
Pause policy: short pause after the opening; full beat before the visual number and before “the real one”
Pronunciation:
- ResonantDAO: resonant dow

## VID-RTC-001

Some messages aren’t meant for today.

## VID-RTC-002

A thought for your future self. A promise to someone you love. A question for people you haven’t met yet. Or an open space, if nothing needs to be said.

## VID-RTC-003

What we do leave becomes a small parcel of light. Different voices, different places, moving toward the same point.

## VID-RTC-004

Alone, each is only one message. Together, they find one another. The paths connect. The parcels become one shared capsule.

## VID-RTC-005

When the capsule closes, it becomes one fingerprint. One public signal joins it. And a number appears: how many tokens ResonantDAO starts with.

## VID-RTC-006

For a moment, a number appears. The real one comes later, from the capsule we shape together.

## VID-RTC-007

The number stays. The messages travel—to two, five, and ten years.

## VID-RTC-008

Then they return. Leave something for later.
