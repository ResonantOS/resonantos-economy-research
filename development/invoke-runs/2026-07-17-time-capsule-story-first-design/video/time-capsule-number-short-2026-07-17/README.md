## Story

One message parcel leaves the present. More parcels appear from different people and
places. Their paths connect and converge inside one shared capsule. The capsule closes,
its shared fingerprint meets one public closing signal, and a number appears.

For one brief beat, that number is **42**. The voice does not name it; it says, “The real
one comes later, from the capsule we shape together.” The easter egg remains discoverable
without posing as the real result. The number stays; the messages continue toward their
2, 5, and 10-year openings.

## Visual Continuity

The film uses the same visual world as the site hero: midnight blue space, restrained
amber and cream light, fine orbital geometry, folded message parcels, and one radiant
circular capsule. It should feel intimate at the beginning, collective in the middle,
and quietly expansive at the end—not cyberpunk, corporate, or crypto-promotional.

## Proof Boundary

This package defines an approved script and production inputs. It does not generate the
video, approve a rendered voice performance, prove audience comprehension, or claim that
42 is the real starting token number.
