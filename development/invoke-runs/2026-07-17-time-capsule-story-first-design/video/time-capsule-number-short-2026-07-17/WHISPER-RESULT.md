# Whisper Result

- Spell: `whisper`
- Transport: `short_initiative_video_model_input_package`
- Transport proof status: `candidate`
- Objective: Create a one-minute, emotionally resonant video that makes the time-capsule
  initiative shareable and visibly connects personal messages to one shared capsule and
  the starting token number.
- Target public: First-time site visitors, ResonantDAO contributors, and people meeting
  the initiative through social sharing.
- SCU cores: Resonance is intimate, connective, quietly cinematic, and non-promotional;
  relevance begins with something a person may want to meet later and makes the shared
  token-number consequence explicit; trajectory moves from one parcel, through many
  connected paths and one capsule, to fingerprint, closing signal, `42`, future returns,
  and invitation.
- Candidate selected: `message-parcels-to-one-capsule`, because the same object can carry
  personal meaning, collective connection, causal explanation, and future return without
  becoming a technical diagram.
- Composition plan: Four acts and eight scenes over 60 seconds: something leaves, many
  become one, a number appears, and the messages continue.
- Draft status: `pass`
- Human gate: `approved`
- Validation: Canonical YAML generated five production projections; eight scenes total
  exactly 60 seconds and 134 words; pacing, source references, visual assets, visual-only
  `42` honesty, causal clarity, and site-embed planning pass. Rendered voice, generated
  media, final edit, and audience learning remain unproven.
- Learning residue: The parcel must remain the continuous explanatory object. The number
  reveal gains impact from stillness, not spectacle. `42` is stronger when discovered
  visually instead of named and explained. The site video deepens but never replaces the
  plain-language explanation.
