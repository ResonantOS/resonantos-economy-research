## Opening

### Projected Copy

Some messages aren’t for today.

### Spoken Copy

Some messages aren’t meant for today.

A thought for your future self. A promise to someone you love. A question for
people you haven’t met yet. Or an open space, if nothing needs to be said.

## Tension

### Projected Copy

Different voices. One direction.

### Spoken Copy

What we do leave becomes a small parcel of light. Different voices,
different places, moving toward the same point.

Alone, each is only one message. Together, they find one another. The paths
connect. The parcels become one shared capsule.

## Reveal

### Projected Copy

One capsule.

One starting number.

### Spoken Copy

When the capsule closes, it becomes one fingerprint. One public signal joins
it. And a number appears: how many tokens ResonantDAO starts with.

For a moment, a number appears. The real one comes later, from the capsule we
shape together.

The number stays. The messages travel—to two, five, and ten years.
Then they return. Leave something for later.
