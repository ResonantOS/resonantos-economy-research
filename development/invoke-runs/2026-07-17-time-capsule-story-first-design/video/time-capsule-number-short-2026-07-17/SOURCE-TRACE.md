## Prohibited Overclaims

- `42` is the real or probable starting token number.
- One message produces a fixed quantity of tokens.
- Participation grants allocation, status, influence, or reward.
- Bitcoin or another public signal stores message content.
- The current site already collects, seals, recovers, or reveals messages.
- A polished video proves the initiative is understood or trusted.
