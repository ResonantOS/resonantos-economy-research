# Design Transport

## Start Here

Open `STORY-PROTOTYPE.html`. Experience the page as a newcomer before reading the
architecture.

## Carry Forward

- One story: present message → shared capsule → ritual number → future return.
- One stable experience card.
- Up to two short messages per 2/5/10-year opening.
- Public now, saved for opening, or quiet.
- Persistent rehearsal boundary.
- Human retell gate before publication.

## Do Not Carry Forward As Approved

- 140-character limit.
- Saved-message storage, encryption, recovery, or custody.
- Exact Bitcoin closing rule or range mapping.
- Live form, database, moderation, identity, deployment, or token effect.
