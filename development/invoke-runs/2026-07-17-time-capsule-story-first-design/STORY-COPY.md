# Leave something for later.

Leave a message or any small mark—for your future self, someone else, or the community.
You can also leave nothing at all. What we do leave shapes the capsule that helps choose
how many tokens ResonantDAO starts with. Anything left can return in 2, 5, or 10 years.

**What we leave → one capsule → the starting token number**

**Leave a message or mark** · **Or leave nothing**

## Future Glimpse

**Two years — Recognize what stayed.** Meet a hope, promise, question, or small detail
you chose to notice today.

**Five years — Notice what changed.** Compare what you believe now with the life,
relationships, and choices that unfolded.

**Ten years — Carry something across.** Find what you decided was worth protecting from
the quiet work of time.

## One Number. Three Returns.

The capsule closes once to help choose the starting token number. Then it stays alive
long enough to return what people left—one horizon at a time.

### 01 — Close one shared moment

What people leave becomes one frozen manifest. Its fingerprint joins a public Bitcoin
closing block, and a rule places the result inside the number range agreed in advance.
No single message receives a value of its own.

```text
messages + marks + saved-message commitments
  -> one frozen manifest
  -> one fingerprint
  + Bitcoin closing block
  -> published range rule
  -> starting token number
```

Candidate mapping shape:

`minimum + (hash(fingerprint + block) mod range size)`

### 02 — Keep three future bundles alive

The public manifest can be copied and checked. Saved messages need one protected release
bundle per horizon, recoverable copies, access shared among independent stewards, and
regular recovery checks.

Candidate starting point—not approved: three independent storage locations, 3-of-5
steward access, and a yearly restore check.

### 03 — Open only what is due

At each date, stewards recover access to that horizon only. The saved message and its
secret proof are published together. Anyone can reproduce the old commitment and confirm
that the message was already inside the frozen capsule. Later bundles remain closed.

**What never changes:** the manifest, fingerprint, and starting number.

**What still needs a decision:** the final mapping rule, encryption and archive format,
storage copies, stewards, succession, and the recovery schedule.

## Message Studio

**What would you like to find later?**

Leave up to two messages or marks for each opening. A mark can be a word, a symbol, or one
small sentence. Write to yourself, to another person, or to everyone. Every place is optional.

## Quiet Path

**Nothing has to be said.**

You can go back and leave any small mark, or keep every opening empty. An open space
belongs here too.

- 2 years: “What do you hope still feels true?”
- 5 years: “What might you understand differently?”
- 10 years: “What deserves to cross a decade?”

## Visibility

**When should this be read?**

**Share from the beginning** — It can be part of the public capsule from the start.

**Save for this opening** — Only a digital commitment enters the public capsule. The
message can be revealed and checked when its opening arrives. This is planned, not live.

## Ritual Bridge

**Now see where your message goes.**

Your message is not given a score. It becomes one record in the shared capsule.

```text
your message
  -> one capsule record
  -> the shared fingerprint
  + the Bitcoin closing block
  -> the starting token number
```

Every accepted message changes the capsule as a whole. The Bitcoin block closes the
moment; it does not store the messages. The starting token number is chosen once. The
capsule continues.

## Return

At each opening, the messages for that moment return. You may meet your own words again;
another person may find something meant for them; the community may revisit what was
shared. Messages saved for the opening are revealed with the small proof that lets anyone
check they are the same messages placed there years before.

## Receipt

**You reached a future opening.**

Nothing was sent. Nothing was saved. But the journey you just saw is the experience we
can build once the real capsule, its care, and its recovery path are ready.
