# Language Audition

## Hero

| Candidate | Note |
| --- | --- |
| “Write the future into the number.” | Too mystical and overstates personal causality. |
| “What should ResonantDAO remember?” | Clear, but sounds like a survey. |
| “Leave something for a later us.” | Warm, but still sounds community-directed. |
| “Leave something you want to meet again.” | Personally open, but too long in the rendered hero. |
| “Leave something for later.” | Selected: short, personal, and open to any recipient. |

## Time Prompts

| Candidate | Note |
