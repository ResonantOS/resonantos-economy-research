# Distill Validation

## Distill Result

- Target context: one first-time participant visit.
- Objective and output: a candidate story-first UX plus isolated review prototype.
- Mode and budget: Standard; one proposal track, two recursive rounds, role simulation.
- Verdict: `pass` for design unit; `flag` for human proof.
- Current smallest coherent unit: **open-address message-to-return journey**.
- Responsibility: let one person leave a short message for themselves, another person,
  or the community; understand how it joins the
  shared ritual, and know when it returns.

## Role Conversation Trace

| Round | Proposer | Balancer objection | Reconciliation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Lead with a future-memory invitation | Meaning may eclipse mechanism | Put the mechanism immediately after the first act of writing |
| 2 | Explain the full commitment and Bitcoin path | Cognitive load is too high for a newcomer | Keep five short visual nodes; move proof detail to disclosure |

Revision check: community-only addressing was rejected because the capsule is shared but
the meaning of each message does not have to be. Opening the addressee preserves the same
inputs, record boundary, ritual path, and future returns without adding protocol complexity.

Layout/copy revision check: the smallest coherent opening is one full-image field containing
the brand, invitation, explanation, and action. Splitting the artwork into a side card made
it decorative instead of structural. Horizon prompts now name the literal year because the
poetic alternatives added interpretation without adding meaning.

Token-number revision check: the personal invitation alone reduced the idea to a generic
time capsule. The smallest coherent opening also needs the causal bridge “your messages →
one capsule → the starting token number,” explained as how many tokens ResonantDAO starts
with. On tall desktop screens, the opening follows the artwork's native wide proportion so
the image remains structural without being stretched to the viewport.

## Closure And Recomposition Proof

The unit has an input (a short message to self, another person, everyone, or quiet), a choice (public now or saved for its
opening), an output (one capsule record), a visible effect (shared fingerprint and starting
token number), and a future destination (2/5/10-year opening). It recomposes into the larger
ritual without requiring the participant to operate storage, cryptography, or Bitcoin.

## Deferred Complexity

Encryption, threshold keys, recovery copies, moderation, accounts, database, Bitcoin
transaction construction, and exact range mapping are deferred because they do not belong
inside the smallest participant design unit.

## Frame Expiry

This optimization point expires if the experience becomes a real submission surface or if
the saved-message capability is killed. Either change requires a new owner-approved design.
