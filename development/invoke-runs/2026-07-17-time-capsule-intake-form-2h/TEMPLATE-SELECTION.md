# Template Selection

- Design profile: compact six-view architecture.
- UX companion: `invoke.ux-plan`, selected because the primary risk is
  comprehension and one-screen interaction.
- Plan companions: standalone `WORK-PACK.md` and
  `IMPLEMENTATION-LAYERING.md`.
- Complexity: medium; the form has independently verifiable semantic,
  interaction, responsive, and validation concerns.
- Output mode: split SWU contracts.
- Full dispatch JSON: skipped. The route is sequential, local, single-owner, has
  no subagents and crosses no protected context boundary.
