## Design Shape

The fixture package requires:

- `README.md` for purpose, source basis, non-goals, and package scope;
- `SCHEMA.md` for required fixture fields;
- `MATRIX.md` for rule-to-fixture coverage;
- `TYPED-NEGATIVES.md` for rejected transitions;
- `IMPLEMENTATION-CHECKLIST.md` for completion and safety checks;
- six fixture files for positive use, invisible work, authority no-effect,
  attention capture, reviewer bottleneck, and appeal/recusal.
