# Number Lottery Presentation — Composition Plan

Status: full composition generated after operator voice approval

Selected candidate: conversational founding invitation with explicit trust tension

## Construction Principle

The presentation is a performed conversation. The screen carries one thought at
a time; the presenter carries context, transitions, evidence, and uncertainty.

## Body Parts

1. **Choice** — show that an arbitrary supply is a rare founding choice.
2. **Contribution** — introduce one public word or one sealed contribution as
   the human material of the number.
3. **Trust** — establish that the set must close before the final signal and
   that public, sealed, and encrypted are different promises.
4. **Time** — connect the number reveal to annual preservation and 2/5/10-year
   reflection moments.
5. **Invitation** — ask the community to help shape the rules and join the
   future cohort, without treating the ceremony as launched.

## Introduction And Ending

- Opening: “Every token begins with a number. Ours can begin with a memory.”
- Narrative anchor: the initial supply becomes the first collective artifact.
- Reveal: the community seals its contributions before the final signal makes
  the number knowable.
- Ending direction after approval: invite rule-shaping and participation, not
  token purchase, private submission, or launch endorsement.

## Audition Validation Checklist

- projected copy is addressed to the room;
- spoken copy reads aloud naturally;
- each moment contains one visible thought;
- no authoring metadata is projected;
- no formal term appears before concrete need;
- no unsupported fairness, privacy, randomness, or engagement claim;
- exactly opening, tension, and reveal are shown;
- full generation remains blocked until explicit operator approval.
