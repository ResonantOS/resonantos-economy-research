# Number Lottery Presentation — Text Intent Substrate

Status: ready for language audition; full presentation blocked pending voice approval
Spell: Whisper
Transport: `live_presentation` (`candidate`)
Mode: guided-auto intake plus Distill Standard

## Intake Decision

- Target public: ResonantDAO community and founding contributors.
- Room outcome: they understand the ritual, feel why it belongs to the founding
  moment, and want to help shape and participate in it.
- Transport: a live presentation supported by projected moments.
- Presenter assumption: a ResonantDAO founder or community steward.
- Duration assumption: short introduction, approximately 8–10 minutes; this is
  non-blocking and may change after the voice gate.
- Success signal: the audience can retell the idea in one sentence and opts into
  the next design/participation step without mistaking the project for launched.

## Resonance Core

- Felt meaning: the initial supply can become a shared memory rather than an
  unexplained parameter.
- Voice: visionary, intimate, direct, lightly mysterious, and grounded.
- Values: collective authorship, plurality, consent, preservation, trust, and
  responsibility to future members.
- Desired residue: “This number can carry us because we made its story together.”
- Forbidden feels: crypto hype, numerology as proof, casino spectacle, forced
  participation, technical theatre, or false certainty.

## Relevance Core

- Audience state: they know ResonantDAO as a community project but may not know
  why the initial supply should become a participatory ritual.
- Central relevance: the founding community has a rare one-time opportunity to
  turn an arbitrary choice into a shared act.
- Authority mode: operator-authorized concept proposal; mechanism and ceremony
  remain unvalidated and unlaunched.
- Likely objections: one person could steer the number; private words may not be
  private; rules could change after submissions; the time capsule could become
  forgotten or coercive.
- Required public distinction: public words, sealed commitments, and encrypted
  content are not the same thing.

## Trajectory Core

- Objective: move the room from “supply is just a parameter” to “the first
  number can be a collective founding artifact worth protecting.”
- Narrative anchor: every token begins with a number; this one can begin with a
  memory.
- Movement: arbitrary choice → meaningful opportunity → trust tension →
  collective reveal → long-term invitation.
- Introduction policy: start with the human choice, not hashing, commitments,
  beacons, fixtures, or governance vocabulary.
- Ending policy: invite participation in shaping the rules and future cohort;
  do not solicit real private content or imply launch approval.

## Smallest Coherent Unit

Name: `Collective Founding Number`

Responsibility: let the room understand that ResonantDAO can transform an
otherwise arbitrary initial supply into a community-authored, reproducible,
time-bound ritual without pretending that meaning alone proves fairness,
privacy, or readiness.

Inputs: the arbitrary-supply decision, public/sealed word concept, trust
boundary, and 2/5/10-year capsule horizon.

Output: a clear invitation the audience can repeat and evaluate.

Recomposition: the unit expands into the complete presentation through five
body parts—choice, contribution, trust, time, and invitation—without requiring
mechanism detail in the opening.

## Distill Role Trace

- Proposer: center the story on the one-time founding opportunity to create the
  initial number together.
- Evidence/assumption: supply is intentionally selectable; the community and
  founding contributors are the named public; participation effects are
  hypotheses, not evidence.
- Balancer objection — cognitive load: a hash/beacon explanation would make the
  audience solve the mechanism before caring about the ritual.
- Balancer objection — unsupported knowledge: “fair,” “private,” “random,” and
  “engaging” cannot be asserted as achieved.
- Reconciliation: use human language in the audition, make the anti-steering and
  public/sealed distinction felt, and reserve implementation vocabulary for
  later presenter surfaces after voice approval.
- Stable tension: the final supply-selection procedure is not validated; the
  presentation can invite co-design but cannot announce a finished draw.

## Frame Expiry

Rerun the substrate if the audience changes, the room becomes an approval vote,
the mechanism is validated or rejected, the public/private design changes, or
the intended call to action becomes real cohort intake.
