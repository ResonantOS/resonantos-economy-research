# Quine Definitional-Soundness Review

Verdict: REVISE with KILLs

| Candidate | Verdict | Definitional finding |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Character/code-point sum | **KILL** | Re-skinned arbitrary encoding; collisions erase distinctions. |
| Public-manifest digest | **REVISE** | Needs exact schema, bytes, cohort, ordering, duplicate, hash, and integer definitions; digest is not random/fair/private/calibrated. |
| Commitment-cohort root | **REVISE** | Commitment requires a named protocol and assumptions; root proves neither humans, consent, secrecy, nor fairness. |
| Hybrid commitment + ciphertext | **REVISE** | Commitment and ciphertext serve different claims; recovery needs named encryption/key/nonce/custody/migration rules. |
| Fixed 1B + ritual labels | **REVISE** | Use commemorative label, not denomination; distinguish planned total, maximum, initial mint, and circulating supply. |
| Externally bounded mapping | **REVISE/DEFER** | Range does not calibrate itself; quantity and bounds require independent policy justification. |
| Direct word-derived supply | **KILL** | Community provenance is being renamed economic calibration. |

## Terminology Constraints

- submission artifact is one accepted versioned record, not a person/wallet;
- encoding provides no secrecy;
- digest is neither encryption nor automatically a commitment;
- commitment names a commit/open construction and hiding/binding assumptions;
- ciphertext names an encryption construction plus key/nonce/data rules;
- cohort root authenticates accepted leaves, not identity or consent;
- symbolic seed has zero supply/reward/credit/reputation/governance effect;
- total, maximum, initially minted, and circulating supply remain distinct;
- participant must distinguish member, wallet, submitter, human, and artifact;
- replace bare private with non-public, encrypted, committed-but-unopened, or
  access-restricted;
- deterministic does not mean random or fair;
- use provenance-bearing or subjectively meaningful, not intrinsic significance;
- community-derived means one admitted cohort under frozen rules, not universal
  legitimacy;
- engagement is an observable event/window hypothesis, not a mentality effect;
- resonance remains normative framing unless separately operationalized.

The ritual-only architecture survives. Direct supply derivation and serious
character-sum derivation do not.
