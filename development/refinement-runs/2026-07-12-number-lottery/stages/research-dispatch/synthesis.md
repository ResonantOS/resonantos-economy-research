## Answer

The strongest candidate picture is a **ritual layer around the already-planned
1,000,000,000 `$RES`**, not a new supply-selection mechanism. The current
ResonantDAO whitepaper fixes 1B as the draft plan; changing it requires an
external token-policy process. The capsule can still produce a meaningful,
reproducible symbolic seed from community provenance, while 2/5/10-year events
create continuity without granting rewards, credit, or authority. [Fritz
§Directly supported claims; Ostrom §Supported claims; Kahneman §Supported
claims; https://resonantdao.com/whitepaper/]

Meaning should come from transparent participation and preservation—not from
claiming that the integer is mathematically, economically, or psychologically
special. [Fritz §Candidate mechanisms; Ostrom §Supported claims; Kahneman
§External-validity limits]

## Recommended Smallest Coherent Unit

A versioned public cohort manifest with:

1. one eligible artifact per admitted participant;
2. public normalized messages or specialist-defined commitments;
3. a frozen ordered cohort root;
4. a reproducible ritual-only integer;
5. explicit public/private/non-participation modes;
6. no token, reward, reputation, or governance effect;
7. 1B `$RES` preserved as current draft policy;
8. encrypted recovery payloads deferred until custody and threat-model owners
   exist.

## Engagement Portfolio

- Annual checksum and preservation drill, plus **Counterfactual Number Day**
  showing how rule choices change outputs.
- 2-year **Then / Now Echo**: explicit openings beside present reflections; no
  automatic publication.
- 5-year **Rule and Divergence Assembly**: audit future-cohort rules, dissent,
  archive recovery, and successor readiness without altering the frozen cohort.
- 10-year **Constellation and Stewardship Handoff**: consent-safe reveals,
  orphan report, no-survivor outcome, and intergenerational dialogue.
- Translation circle, newcomer response cohort, counter-capsule audit, and
  anti-capture red team; none creates status rankings or economic benefits.

## Required Toy Witnesses

Normalization/collision; duplicate and 100-wallet Sybil; last-submitter
grinding; modulo bias; harmful-content quarantine without root rewriting;
privacy-cue comprehension; key/nonce/opening loss; steward disappearance and
migration; silence/death defaulting to non-reveal; narrative/status capture;
multilingual fixtures; and a hard economic-boundary test proving ritual output
cannot modify 1B supply or authority.

## Typed Negatives

- `integer encoding != encryption`
- `hash != validated commitment`
- `Merkle inclusion != unique human or consent`
- `determinism != fairness`
- `participation != authority`
- `community provenance != economic calibration`
- `symbolic seed != safe token supply`
- direct supply coupling is rejected locally.

## Contradictions And Residue

- Augmentatism’s plural “Many” conflicts with one privileged language, cipher,
  or mandatory disclosure.
- Public verifiability conflicts with confidentiality; commitments prove neither
  identity nor consent, while encryption introduces custody and loss.
- Long-term memory ritual conflicts with consent drift and unreliable
  recollection; original artifact and present interpretation must remain
  separate.
- Founder meaning can become informal aristocracy even with zero formal
  authority.
- Exact 2/5/10-year effects, DAO participation benefits, cryptographic safety,
  and economic safety remain unvalidated.
