## Constraints

- No token-supply number may be selected or described as economically safe.
- No cryptographic security claim without a named construction, threat model,
  and primary technical source.
- No real capsule content or personal data is collected.
- No participation, contribution, word count, commitment, or token amount
  changes governance authority.
- Bounded research is one comparison pass over the three supplied public sites
  plus a small primary technical corpus.
- Existing dirty files outside this run are preserved; owner files change only
  through their explicit hooks.

  result agree.
