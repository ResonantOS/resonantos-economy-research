## Result

The confirmed five-lane dispatch rejects the original `investment` and
`cashing` metaphor as a mechanism. A narrower candidate survives: an ER-D5
prior-activity receipt may support a contestable claim linking it to one later
witnessed result; contextual review may accept, reject, or hold the claim; an
accepted claim may create one result-scoped ER-D7 Reviewed Credit Record. The
pipeline ends there. It creates no reward, payout, reputation, policy,
governance, implementation, or authority effect.

No admitted source owns, deploys, or validates the integrated pipeline.
E1–E4 now have proposed positive, negative, and held cases for governance
calls, directed study, mentoring/maintenance, and cross-domain transfer. They
are not instantiated or run. Preventive work (E5) remains a separate bounded
prior-art sweep.
