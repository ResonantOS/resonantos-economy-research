## P1 Trigger Decision

Research dispatch is justified by synthesis, context protection, isolation, and
parallelism: four external source corpora must be collected independently and
reconciled with the official-source constraint. Review dispatch is justified by
the size and risk of the eventual multi-artifact target and the need for
independent full-corpus attack lenses.
