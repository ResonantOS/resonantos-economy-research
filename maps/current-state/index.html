Reader terms

These are plain-language aids for reading this x-ray. They do not create or promote canonical repository definitions.

Closed research

Means: the bounded investigation finished. Does not mean: its mechanism works.

Unrunnable

Means: a required object, state, input, comparison, or witness is absent. It is not neutral evidence.

KILL

Means: reject the named proposition for its recorded reason. Do not widen it to every adjacent question.

Human gate

Means: the proposed dispatch needs explicit confirmation. Readiness is not authorization.

Witness

Means: inspectable evidence from the declared run or workflow. A document existing is not a result witness.

Promotion

Means: a separate authority decision. Positive evidence may inform it but never performs it automatically.
