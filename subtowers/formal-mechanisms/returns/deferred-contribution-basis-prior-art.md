## Bounded Finding

Formal work supplies calculable read models and adversarial sensitivity tests,
not a socially legitimate contribution score. This dispatch therefore retains
only non-aggregating result-link case sets, vector views, contested edge/joint-
dependency graphs, and result-centred dossiers. Person totals, rankings, caps,
decay, concavity, and half-lives remain excluded until a named use, unit, owner,
and witness exist.
