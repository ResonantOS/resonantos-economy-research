# Tower Plan

Status: scaffolded
Promotion scope: local-research-only

## Objective

Find formal mechanism candidates for conserved credit, anti-collusion, randomized audits, signal separation, reputation decay, and stress thresholds.

## Closure Standard

- Each mechanism names its invariant.
- Each invariant names its witness or evidence-harness need.
- Randomness is assigned to a purpose, not used as a vague fairness tool.
- No mechanism becomes token policy or implementation.
