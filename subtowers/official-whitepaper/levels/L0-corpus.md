## Initial Source Notes

- Phase 1 is contribution-first and before algorithmic financial tokens.
- `$RES` is a transferable reward and utility token with no external liquidity at launch.
- `$RCT` is non-transferable contribution reputation.
- Long-term governance is contextual, not one-token-one-vote.
- AI agents may participate economically where allowed but should not receive human-only governance power.
- Phase 2 financial mechanisms are future, gated, and risk-sensitive.
