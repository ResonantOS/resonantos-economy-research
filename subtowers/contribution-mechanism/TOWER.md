# Tower Plan

Status: bounded source synthesis accepted; review and evidence handoff pending
Depth: full, bounded by dispatch quotas
Promotion scope: local-research-only

## Research Question

How can a DAO describe heterogeneous contributions, preserve evidence of prior
contribution, review its connection to later witnessed results, learn from
informative failures, and allocate future opportunities without collapsing
planning estimates, contribution records, result recognition, reward, or
authority into one score?

## Importance And Collapse Test

This is a cross-cutting research program because its decisions affect every
candidate contribution family and all three existing deferred-contribution
bets. Its importance would narrow sharply if bounded research and representative
shadow-versus-consequential experiments found that contribution proxies do not
materially change behavior, result-link accuracy, exclusion, concentration, or
review burden when they begin controlling recognition or opportunity.

## Compositional Spine

```text
descriptive contribution record
  -> planning-only dimensions
  -> witnessed output or result evidence
  -> result-specific linkage claim
  -> conflict-aware review
  -> accepted | rejected | held
  -> bounded result-scoped record
  -> no automatic reward, reputation, or authority
```

Opportunity allocation is a parallel decision informed by current capability,
availability, conflicts, workload, access goals, and relevant history. It does
not inherit a result-recognition verdict automatically.

## Two-Language Research Contract

| Participant-facing phrase | Analytical phrase | Research risk |
| --- | --- | --- |
| contribution investment | language-test stimulus mapped to a witnessed prior contribution record | KILL as mechanism field; may imply stored value or debt |
| realization / cashing | language-test stimulus mapped to a result-link disposition | KILL as mechanism field; may imply conversion, money, or entitlement |
| witnessed result | result with declared acceptance evidence | may hide contestable quality or causation |
| provisional filtered lottery | separately tested eligibility gate and mechanical random order | may hide corruption of the eligible pool or launder fairness |

Every public or training artifact must either pair the two languages or state
which one it uses. The research dispatch must test comprehension rather than
assume the bridge is safe.

## What The Tower Must Compare

- task/contribution families without a universal value ladder;
- planning dimensions versus result dimensions;
- scalar scores versus vectors, dossiers, graphs, and reference cases;
- activity evidence versus result-link evidence;
- history-ranked versus conflict-filtered or randomized review assignment;
- history-ranked, qualified-lottery, rotation, and newcomer-access allocation;
- successful results, informative failures, inconclusive attempts, execution
  defects, and preventive contribution;
- metrics in shadow use versus the same metrics made consequential;
- a first-order anti-gaming defense versus the new target created by that
  defense.

## Named Candidate Experiments

The workshop's six candidates remain unregistered and unrun. Research changed
their shapes:

1. vector measurement -> split into description, planning, and review; kill the
   omnibus superiority claim;
2. reviewer independence -> split conflict, recusal, qualification, masking,
   assignment, disposition, and appeal;
3. opportunity allocation -> split C1 gate/access from C2 filtered-draw
   mechanics;
4. allocation inflation -> retain only one objective-proxy-benefit-defense
   contract per run;
5. informative failure -> kill current circular CM-D5 and rebuild prospective
   E1/E2; and
6. newcomer-plus-experienced pairing -> future work until definitions and
   bounded evidence exist.

The accepted findings propose no new Goodhart meta-bet. CM-Q6 remains a
cross-cutting shadow, visible-status, and scarce-benefit experiment contract.
Research cannot register or run an experiment by itself.

## Closure Standard

The tower is not closed until:

- each admitted source has source kind, supported claim, unsupported inference,
  local bridge, and bridge risk;
- each track has a borrow/block/future-work decision;
- the investment/realization language has a comprehension and entitlement-risk
  finding;
- every consequential metric has an objective, proxy, optimizer, benefit
  connection, failure path, and defense-pressure question;
- reviewer conflict and randomness name what is protected and what must never be
  randomized;
- failure value is separated from failure counts;
- history is separated from entitlement and authority;
- research and review subagent lanes have explicit closeout;
- empirical blockers are handed to `research-evidence-harness`; and
- no artifact claims the mechanism is validated or ready for policy.
