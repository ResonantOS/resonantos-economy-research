# Candidate Tower Definitions

Status: research-revised local candidates; not registered under ER-D23
Promotion scope: tower discussion only

These definitions prevent the scaffold from using the same word in several
incompatible ways. They do not create canonical economy terms.

## CM-D1 Contribution Description

**Candidate form:** a record of what happened, its family, context, intended
recipient, and output evidence.

**Intuition:** describe the contribution before deciding what it enabled.

**Blocked collapse:** description must not imply result value, recognition,
reward, allocation priority, or authority.

**Research verdict:** REVISE/SPLIT. A contributor confirmation establishes
declaration provenance only. A fixture must distinguish activity/role
description from recognized contribution.

## CM-D2 Planning Vector

**Candidate form:** an unscalarized set of ex-ante ranges for effort,
uncertainty, dependencies, difficulty, coordination, error risk, reversibility,
and verification cost.

**Intuition:** make planning disagreements visible without manufacturing one
person-value number.

**Blocked collapse:** vector components may not be summed or compared across
families without an explicit, reviewed purpose and weighting rule.

**Research verdict:** REVISE. Description, planning, and result review require
different vectors, objectives, comparators, and collapse bounds. The omnibus
claim that vectors are better is KILL.

## CM-D3 Contribution Investment

**Candidate participant-facing form:** a witnessed prior contribution record
retained because it may later become relevant to a witnessed result.

**Analytical equivalent:** prior contribution record with no presumed result
link.

**Blocked collapse:** it is not stored value, credit balance, DAO liability,
reward promise, reputation, or authority.

**Research verdict:** KILL as a mechanism field or analytical alias. The phrase
may appear only as a stimulus in the independent `BET-PRZ-06-a` language-safety
test.

## CM-D4 Result-Specific Realization

**Candidate form:** a conflict-aware review disposition (`accepted`,
`rejected`, or `held`) on one result-link claim connecting a prior contribution
record to one witnessed result.

**Language-test stimuli only:** realization or cashing; neither is an operational
mechanism field.

**Blocked collapse:** acceptance does not create money, a reusable person
balance, global rank, future entitlement, governance influence, or causal proof.

**Research verdict:** KILL `realization` and `cashing` as mechanism aliases;
REVISE the underlying result-link disposition candidate without those economic
meanings.

## CM-D5 Informative Failure

**Candidate form:** a credible attempt with preserved evidence and a witnessed
negative result that materially changes a hypothesis, option, constraint, or
later decision.

**Blocked collapse:** attempt count, non-delivery, execution defect, duplicate
failure, or safety breach is not informative failure by default.

**Research verdict:** KILL the current definition as circular because later
decision change both admits the case and supplies the proposed outcome. A
replacement must freeze prospective dossier eligibility before E2 observes any
later decision, duplicate-attempt, or use outcome.

## CM-D6 Qualified Pool

**Candidate form:** a time-bounded set of eligible people after current
capability, availability, conflict, workload, and safety requirements are
applied for one named opportunity.

**Blocked collapse:** prior recognition or global reputation cannot establish
standing membership or authority.

**Research verdict:** REVISE and provisionally rename the procedure a filtered
pool/draw. Eligibility satisfaction is not merit, and a random draw is not
fairness.

## CM-D7 Consequential Proxy

**Candidate form:** a measure that influences recognition, eligibility,
allocation, a prize decision, or another scarce benefit.

**Intuition:** the same measure can behave differently as a description and as
a target.

**Blocked collapse:** observed correlation before activation is not evidence
that the relationship survives optimization pressure.

**Research verdict:** RETAIN/REVISE only with one frozen objective, proxy,
optimizer, controlled benefit, defense proxy, harm surface, and numeric collapse
threshold per run.

## Result-Link Disposition Candidate

- `accepted`: one claim passes frozen evidence criteria for one named result; it
  is not causal truth, value, merit, or future qualification.
- `rejected`: the evidence contradicts or does not support the declared link;
  no result-scoped record is created.
- `held`: declared uncertainty prevents disposition; it requires a reason,
  expiry, missing-evidence list, re-review owner, and criteria version. Expiry
  creates neither a record nor automatic acceptance.

Every disposition has reward, payout, reputation, policy, governance, and
authority effects set to `none`.

## Definition Lifecycle Handoff

Before fixture or schema use, the owner must decide whether CM-D1-CM-D7 map to
existing ER definitions, require new ER-D23 candidates, or should be killed.
