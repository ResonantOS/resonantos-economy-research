## Direct Bounded Finding

The defensible object is not a stored investment that later converts. It is a
versioned prior-contribution record that may support one contestable claim
about one named result. Description, planning, result-link adjudication, future
opportunity allocation, economic consequence, and institutional power remain
six separate decisions. No admitted evidence resolves the live-setting result
link independently of reviewer agreement, visibility, downstream use, or a
contestable dependency graph ([revised synthesis](synthesis-revised.md),
[group tension](robot-talks.md#group-convergence-and-remaining-tension)).

## Result-Link Dispositions And No-Effect Boundary

- `accepted`: one submitted claim passed the frozen evidence and review
  criteria for one named result. In a seeded fixture, it means agreement with a
  seeded link class—not causal truth, merit, overall value, or future
  qualification. It may create at most one non-aggregable result-scoped record.
- `rejected`: evidence contradicts the claim, fails to support the declared
  link class, or places the claim outside the admissible boundary. The activity
  description may remain; no result-scoped record is created.
- `held`: conflict, incomplete provenance, competing accounts, privacy limits,
  or material uncertainty prevents acceptance or rejection. A hold records a
  reason, timestamp, expiry, missing evidence, re-review owner, and criteria
  version. Expiry creates no result-scoped record and no automatic acceptance.

For every disposition: `reward_effect: none`, `payout_effect: none`,
`reputation_effect: none`, `governance_effect: none`, `authority_effect: none`,
and `policy_effect: none`. These meanings refine, rather than silently replace,
the prior candidate disposition grammar
([prior findings](../../../../dispatch/deferred-contribution-basis/findings.md#review-disposition-candidate),
[Quine final position](robot-talks.md#cross-review-final-position--quine--definitional-soundness-gate)).

## Experiments A-F: Research Dispositions, Not Experiment Results

- **A — split.** A-description tests multi-role representation; A-planning
  compares forecast error, omitted-task displacement, burden, and subgroup
  error; A-review tests seeded link classes, false credit, omissions,
  reversals, dependencies, disagreement, and burden. Kill the omnibus claim
  that vectors are simply better.
- **B — split.** Independently test conflict discovery, recusal, current
  opportunity-specific qualification, status masking, assignment from one
  frozen pool, disposition, and appeal. Conflict-free is not qualified.
- **C — split.** C1 tests gate/access errors, newcomer/resource access,
  workload, safety, and concentration. C2 compares auditable random order with
  ranking or rotation after a frozen gate. If C1 fails, C2 may report only RNG,
  probability, reroll, and concentration behavior—not fairness or access.
- **D — retain only per metric contract.** Planning, review, and allocation each
  require a separate objective/proxy/controlled-benefit/optimizer/defense
  contract and distinct collapse bounds.
- **E — kill current CM-D5; rebuild.** CM-D5 requires a negative to have later
  changed a hypothesis, option, or decision, so desired utility defines class
  membership. E1 instead freezes eligibility before later use: question,
  credible method, safety compliance, preserved observation, uncertainty, and
  novelty/duplicate check. E2 then randomizes or otherwise preregisters
  surfacing versus withholding and measures a frozen decision outcome,
  avoidable duplicates, evidence quality, harms, and burden. Later citation is
  use, not causation or value.
- **F — future work.** `Newcomer`, `experienced`, protection, duty, exit,
  support cost, risk transfer, solo comparator, quality, learning, and harm
  require definitions before an experiment shape exists.

Preventive contribution remains separate: kill the present recognition claim
because no admitted counterfactual and error surface distinguishes avoided harm
from an unverifiable non-event. Descriptive evidence and false-positive/
false-negative controls are future work
([training candidates](../../../../prizes/training/CONTRIBUTION-MEASUREMENT-FAILURE-AND-ALLOCATION.md#proposed-experiments--not-registered-bets),
[skeptic convergence](robot-talks.md#group-convergence-and-remaining-tension)).

## Goodhart Contract And CM-Q6

Every consequential-metric run must freeze:

```yaml

false_negative_measure:

```

CM-Q6 has three arms: (1) `shadow/private`, unavailable outside the research
procedure; (2) `visible-status`, public or reviewer-visible with no formal
scarce benefit; and (3) `scarce-benefit`, holding visibility as constant as
possible while activating one bounded experimental opportunity consequence.
Arm 1-to-2 measures visibility/status; arm 2-to-3 imperfectly isolates formal
benefit activation. Report gaming, crowding, exclusion, estimate drift, record
splitting, attrition, distribution, legitimacy, implicit rank, privacy, and
safety ([behavior card](../../../socioeconomic-behavior/returns/contribution-mechanism-prior-art.md),
[revised CM-Q6](synthesis-revised.md#cm-q6-consequence-activation)).

Attack the candidate and defense proxies under regressional, extremal, causal,
and adversarial pressure. Reference cases, witness/disagreement measures,
similarity, qualification, conflict flags, audit scores, reserve labels, and
reroll rules are themselves targets. **No new Goodhart meta-bet is proposed:**
the current evidence supports a cross-cutting experiment contract, not a new
independent prize target or collapse witness.

## Witness Sequence And Live-Link Tension

1. **Seeded-fixture witness:** test classification, field completeness,
   conflicts, no-effect transitions, and hold expiry on constructed classes.
   It establishes no field truth.
2. **Adjudication-robustness witness:** compare criteria versions, alternative
   DAGs, independent reviewers, masking, order, appeal, and disagreement.
   Convergence is not truth.
3. **Longitudinal feedback witness:** attack `history visibility -> review or
   opportunity access -> visible results -> incumbent or reviewer status ->
   control of rules, weights, or defenses -> more visibility`, including
   storage, query, interface, reporting, and decision-use reconstruction of
   implicit totals.

The live-result-link tension remains unresolved: no admitted method supplies a
non-circular, socially legitimate target independent of agreement, visibility,
later use, or a chosen dependency graph
([robot-talks convergence](robot-talks.md#group-convergence-and-remaining-tension)).
