## Evidence Boundary

The integrated mechanism remains undeployed, unvalidated, and without an
admitted precedent owner. The official whitepaper owns only the activity
vocabulary, attendance warning, non-investment boundary, and unresolved
validation/anti-farming questions. It supplies no prior-activity-to-result link,
aggregation rule, review grammar, or downstream authority effect.

The four explorer returns support research fixtures built from owned
components, not the import of external effects:

- failed DAO/crypto precedents show that activity graphs are weight-sensitive
  and farmable, incumbent-selected review access can reproduce networks, peer
  allocation raises reciprocity questions, and concentration alone proves
  neither merit nor capture (`research.md`, Agent 1);
- science-of-science precedents show that typed multi-role records can improve
  description while omitting or falsifying labour, prestige affects visible
  review, delayed attention is not causation, and negative results can vanish
  before review (`research.md`, Agent 2);
- formal precedents show that vectors, contested DAGs, constrained random
  matching, qualified lotteries, and split audit arms are computable while
  their dimensions, graphs, gates, scores, and risk models remain contestable
  proxies (`research.md`, Agent 3); and
- behavioral precedents show no universal incentive effect: consequences can
  induce strategic adaptation or norm change, payment can crowd participation
  in a subgroup, symbolic recognition can increase short-term output, visible
  status can bias review, and lottery legitimacy can depend on framing and
  personal outcome (`research.md`, Agent 4).

## Candidate Mechanism Picture

1. Retain non-exclusive contribution families as descriptions, not value
   ranks. Add hybrid, omitted, honorary, invisible, and joint contribution
   cases plus contributor confirmation.
2. Keep planning and result dimensions separate. Ex-ante effort, uncertainty,
   difficulty, dependencies, coordination, risk, reversibility, and
   verification cost support forecasting only. Ex-post quality, result-link
   class, evidence confidence, joint dependency, durability, and observed
   consequence support one result-specific review. Do not scalarize people,
   teams, families, decisions, or time windows.
3. Preserve the accepted demotion of `investment`, `realization`, and `cashing`
   as mechanism-pipeline aliases. Split out an independent communication test
   comparing operational-only wording to bilingual wording. Improved
   comprehension cannot validate recognition or community utility.
4. Retain the no-effect sequence: prior record -> one witnessed result ->
   contestable link -> conflict-aware `accepted | rejected | held` review -> at
   most one result-scoped record -> end. Dossiers, provenance traces,
   contributor-confirmed roles, alternative graphs, and complementarity remain
   evidence, not person totals.
5. Split informative negative contribution from preventive contribution. An
   informative negative needs a frozen question, credible attempt, preserved
   evidence, witnessed negative, and actual hypothesis, option, or decision
   change. Counts, defects, duplicates, breaches, and cheap repeats do not
   qualify. Preventive avoided-harm contribution remains future work because no
   admitted return supplies a socially valid counterfactual.
6. Separate reviewer conflict eligibility, current qualification, and
   assignment. Randomness may protect assignment unpredictability and raise
   the cost of targeting a reviewer. It must not randomize conflict removal,
   competence, safety, evidence disposition, reward, or authority.
7. History may challengeably evidence current capability, workload, conflicts,
   and repeated exposure. It cannot create standing eligibility, rank,
   entitlement, or authority. Split qualified-pool validity from lottery
   operation and measure legitimacy before and after outcomes.
8. Preserve three independent bets: recognition utility (`BET-PRZ-04-a`),
   community utility (`BET-PRZ-05-a`), and communication safety
   (`BET-PRZ-06-a`). All remain open and evidence cannot be inherited among
   them.

## Exact Evidence-Harness Blockers

1. Reconcile/register the tower's candidate definitions under ER-D23.
2. Freeze one objective, proxy, primary measure, distributional harms, collapse
   threshold, and invalidity rule per experiment.
3. Instantiate positive, negative, held, duplicate, conflict, omission,
   honorary, reciprocal, hidden-conflict, and cheap-failure controls.
4. Define result-link ground truth or adjudication without treating a DAG,
   role label, or reviewer agreement as truth.
5. Freeze inclusion, qualification, conflict, workload, safety, assignment
   probabilities, rerolls, appeal, and data-minimization/deletion rules.
6. Establish pool feasibility and current qualification without global
   reputation or history entitlement.
7. Supply Candidate E's missing failure-learning evidence and Candidate F's
   newcomer/resource evidence; preventive work needs its own sweep.
8. Preserve separate risk-selected and random/stratified audit arms.
9. Keep the three bets' evidence separate and ordered without inheritance.
10. Preserve all `none` effects and obtain a separate reviewed evidence-harness
    handoff after both research and review dispatches close.

Existing typed negatives remain binding: signal -> credit without review;
credit -> reward; credit -> authority; attention -> governance; fixture pass ->
economy validated; fixture package -> implementation readiness; reviewer status
-> standing authority. Candidate additions for skeptic review are: role label ->
value; history/time -> merit; conflict-free -> qualified; qualified draw -> fair;
failure count -> informative failure; missing trace -> zero contribution;
citation/use -> causation; proxy improvement -> objective improvement.

Visible residue includes bounty optimization, Sybil cost, privacy/redaction,
hidden reciprocity, long-run status feedback, multilingual framing, bots,
