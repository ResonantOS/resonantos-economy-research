## Bridge Status Vocabulary

- `borrow-carefully`: source supports a bounded representation or question.
- `analogy-only`: source sharpens a hypothesis but cannot support local effect.
- `block`: source or mechanism should not be used for the proposed inference.
- `future-work`: relevant but outside the bounded cohort.
- `promotion-candidate`: requires a separate owner decision; never automatic.

The cited map is a bounded research result, not a closed corpus or local effect
witness. The review dispatch must attack its source and bridge fidelity.
