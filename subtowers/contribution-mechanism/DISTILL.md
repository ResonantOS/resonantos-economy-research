# Distilled Operator Model

Status: bounded research distill; review pending

## One-Sentence Model

Keep contribution description, planning, result-specific recognition,
opportunity allocation, reward, and authority as separate decisions, then test
how every proxy changes once it becomes consequential.

## Smallest Useful Chain

```text
earlier versioned contribution description
  -> no automatic value, balance, or rank
  -> later witnessed result
  -> claimed evidence link
  -> independent conflict-aware review
  -> accepted | rejected | held-with-expiry
  -> no automatic downstream effect
```

## What To Borrow Carefully

- local, family-specific estimation and reference cases;
- vectors and dossiers that preserve dimensions and disagreement;
- conflict declaration, recusal, current qualification checks, filtered pools,
  bounded random assignment,
  appeal, and concentration audits;
- prospectively eligible negative-result dossiers with preserved evidence;
- Goodhart variants as diagnoses of different proxy failures.

## What To Keep Analogy-Only

- story points as contribution value;
- academic citations as DAO credit;
- grant lotteries as proof of DAO fairness;
- reputation as proof of reviewer independence;
- downstream reuse as a safe attribution primitive.

## What To Block

- person totals and universal contribution rankings;
- automatic accumulation or conversion;
- failure counts as learning value;
- claimant-selected reviewers;
- randomness before qualification and conflict filtering;
- history as entitlement;
- metrics, credit, or reputation becoming governance authority;
- any claim of mechanism validation from prior-art synthesis.

## Staged Witness Boundary

```text
seeded classification fixture
  -> adjudication robustness across criteria, graphs, reviewers, and appeals
  -> longitudinal status, access, and defense-proxy feedback attack
```

No stage alone supplies a live result-link truth or integrated-effect witness.
