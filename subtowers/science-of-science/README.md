# Science Of Science Subtower

Status: scaffolded
Promotion scope: local-research-only

## Boundary

This subtower covers scientometrics, bibliometrics, science of science, peer review, credit allocation, priority dynamics, novelty, replication, and institutional knowledge production.

It must not copy academia as a DAO model. It should extract warnings and mechanisms about how credit systems shape long-term inquiry.

## Research Questions

- How do credit systems alter what people choose to work on?
- What does bibliometrics teach about gaming, prestige, citation cartels, novelty pressure, and maintenance neglect?
- Which lessons support a contribution economy without turning the DAO into academia cosplay?
