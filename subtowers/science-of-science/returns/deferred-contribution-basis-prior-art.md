## Bounded Finding

Typed, contestable, result-specific records are plausible descriptive
precedents. The corpus supports neither study time nor attendance as
contribution, and neither delayed recognition nor role labels prove causation.
General-capacity activity must remain categorically separate from
result-scoped reviewed credit.
