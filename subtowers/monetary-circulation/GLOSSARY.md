# Local Glossary

Status: candidate vocabulary for this tower
Promotion scope: local-research-only

| Term | Local reading | Source kind | Misuse warning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| unit status axes | separate existence, controller, availability, and denomination fields | local synthesis informed by MC-S7 | events and derived views are not statuses |
| typed event | a mint, transfer, disbursement, unlock, fee, reward, or removal transition with provenance and clock | local synthesis | not a chosen schedule or policy |
| typed clocks | accounting, emission, vesting, obligation, execution, and observation time bases with an explicit crosswalk | local synthesis | no universal epoch is assumed |
| holdings distribution | balances over a declared actor-resolution rule and horizon | formal/empirical precedent plus local synthesis | not access, contribution, merit, or authority |
| access distribution | functional access over a declared cohort, route, time, and cost boundary | local synthesis | not holdings or fairness by itself |
| treasury | a declared institutional/control boundary containing positions and obligations | candidate ER-D24 repair | not reserve value or spendable capacity |
| operating outflow | an asset transfer or expenditure used to meet an obligation | local synthesis | never use as a synonym for `token_burn` |
| token burn | irreversible token destruction/removal from supply | protocol precedent plus local synthesis | cannot pay an obligation |
| liquidity | executable exchange capacity under a declared market structure and state | related-source question | not synonymous with supply, price, volume, or headline pool value |
| exit capacity | the amount that can be converted within declared price-impact and time-to-cash bounds | local-inference | not a promise that every holder can exit simultaneously |
| utility | observed use of the token or system for a declared function | primary-source surface plus open residue | not inferred from price or trading activity |
| sink | a rule or use that removes, locks, spends, or otherwise changes availability | local-inference | not automatically healthy demand or value creation |
| typed feedback edge | a source/target relation with sign, delay, institution, evidence, intervention, counterfactual, and predicate | local synthesis from external loop precedents | generic “reflexivity” is killed as one experiment |
| evidence status | per-object result: observed fail, within declared bound, indeterminate, unrunnable, or not applicable | skeptic-reviewed local synthesis | no top-level evidence status exists |
| MC-R | explicitly incomplete record of scoped object reports and typed relations | local synthesis | not a vector, score, health verdict, or completeness claim |
| governor | a feedback mechanism proposed to keep a declared variable within a bound under a stated shock envelope | formal precedent plus local-inference | naming a governor does not show that it works or is legitimate |
