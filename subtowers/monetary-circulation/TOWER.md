# Tower Plan

Status: bounded source research accepted; local evidence work pending
Depth: full, bounded by a separately confirmed dispatch
Promotion scope: local-research-only

## Research Question

Under what supply, distribution, treasury, demand, and market conditions could
`$RES` circulate without producing exhaustion, concentration, extraction
pressure, or reflexive collapse?

## Importance And Collapse Test

This is a program extension because stock, flow, distribution, treasury, and
liquidity risks currently appear in several artifacts without one research
owner. Its contribution would collapse to zero if the bounded source sweep
shows that the existing formal-mechanisms and failed-system findings already
cover every named residue, fixture input, bridge decision, and operator question
without loss or contradiction. In that case, this tower should be replaced by
cross-links rather than maintained as a duplicate.

## Repaired Object Spine

```text
unit/status objects + typed events + typed clocks
  -> separate schedule and allocation objects
  -> holdings, access, and newcomer-acquisition reports
  -> treasury positions, obligations, capacity, and runway reports
  -> route-indexed exit-capacity reports
  -> service-use, persistence, displacement, and sink reports
  -> typed relation edges among declared objects
  -> per-object evidence status or visible residue
  -> no top-level verdict or downstream policy/authority effect
```

## Measurement Contract

The tower must report an explicitly incomplete `MC-R` record rather than a
vector or aggregate health score. Every object report states separately:

- accounting consistency;
- schedule affordability;
- concentration and access distribution;
- treasury runway assumptions;
- liquidity and exit capacity;
- utility or sink dependence;
- its units, clock, boundary, dependencies, predicate, predicate provenance,
  uncertainty, and evidence status; and
- known exclusions and open relation residue.

Passing one dimension cannot compensate for failure in another. A liquid market
does not prove treasury solvency; a fixed supply does not prove sustainable
flows; a positive price does not prove utility; and a long runway does not prove
fair distribution.

## Comparison Obligations

Research must compare, rather than merely describe:

- total supply versus circulating and liquid supply;
- fixed stock versus sustainable flow;
- finite-horizon benefit versus long-horizon concentration;
- internal utility versus external market liquidity;
- native-token treasury value versus non-native spendable reserves;
- reported liquidity versus exit capacity under stress;
- genuine utility demand versus subsidized or speculative demand; and
- open-loop targets versus feedback-governed controls.

## Closure Standard

The source-research stage closed only because:

- every admitted source has a source kind, supported claim, unsupported
  inference, local bridge, and disanalogy;
- existing Lane 3 and Lane 5 findings are reconciled without silently reversing
  their demotions;
- every candidate has a retain/revise/split/kill/future-work disposition;
- every candidate quantity used in a formula or fixture has a tracked
  definition and explicit basis;
- numeric thresholds are either sourced and bridged or remain parameters;
- every liquidity proposal names who supplies capacity, what shock it protects
  against, and what it must not claim;
- every monetary metric remains separate from credit, reputation, governance,
  and authority;
- empirical blockers are handed to `research-evidence-harness`; and
- no artifact claims a validated economy, policy, implementation, or launch.

The tower itself remains open for institutional grounding and evidence work.
