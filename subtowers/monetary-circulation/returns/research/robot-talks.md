## Independent Initial Position — Ostrom / Precedent And Scope

The synthesis is directionally strong as a research map, but it overstates
ownership in two places: the claim that the tower is now a “useful research
owner,” and the claim that every MC-E candidate has an “owned external
witness.” The corpus establishes component precedents and transferable question
forms—not one external owner for the integrated ResonantOS monetary-circulation
problem, and not local witnesses.

- `MC-E1`, `MC-E4`, and `MC-E5` have well-owned methods that can be retained
  with strict local-input gates.
- `MC-E2`, `MC-E3`, and especially `MC-E6` combine objects whose owners and
  assumptions differ; they must be split before handoff.
- `MC-E7` is a useful research question, but “utility integrity” is not directly
  owned by the admitted evidence; the sources own narrower adoption, incentive,
  displacement, and service-use distinctions.
- The seven-component non-compensatory vector is a defensible local organizing
  device, not an empirically established monetary-resilience model.

1. Accounting precedent is stronger than mechanism precedent. EIP-1559 and UNI

2. Schedule literature is assumption-partitioned. The sources are
   non-comparable until reward basis, compounding, recipient process, horizon,
   and participation assumptions match.
3. Treasury cases require an explicit claimant and obligation. Without a
   declared `$RES` obligation, solvency and runway are fixture forms, not local
   observations.
4. Liquidity is institutionally supplied. A fixture must name the providers,
   withdrawal rights, and execution institution; “the market” is not an actor.
5. Diamond–Dybvig run language is analogy-only without sequential service, par
   redemption, and an intermediary holding illiquid assets.
6. Behavioral sources own signs only within their bounded institutions; they do
   not establish the sign of incentives for repeated DAO contribution.

Demotions requested:

  did not perform an exhaustive ownership sweep.”
- “Each candidate has an owned external witness” becomes: “Each candidate has

  local monetary-circulation witness.”
- “TVL and volume can remain high while active depth disappears” becomes: “The

3. Does “exit-depth run” collapse traded-market impact into a Diamond–Dybvig run

## Independent Initial Position — Quine / Definitional Soundness

The synthesis has the right anti-collapse posture but not yet a sound ontology.
Several purported states are different kinds of thing: ownership labels,
availability conditions, events, provenance, and derived observations.
`burn` is dangerously overloaded between token destruction and operating
outflow. `F_feedback` is a relation layer over the other objects, not a seventh
peer dimension. “Terminal failure” also conflates substantive and epistemic
failure.

| MC-E2 release-schedule comparison | SPLIT | Separate mint, treasury disbursement, unlock/vesting, token removal, recipient/allocation, compounding, and obligation-funding paths. The source results cannot share one verdict. |
| MC-E3 concentration trajectory | SPLIT | Separate holdings and access distributions with actor-resolution and cohort rules. Remove “reward per useful unit” until a service/output unit exists. |

| MC-E6 reflexive feedback | SPLIT / FUTURE-WORK; KILL aggregate | Retain typed loop instances. Exact zeroing fact: without endpoints, signs, delays, shock, and failure predicate, generic positive feedback distinguishes no candidate. Make `F` a relation graph. |
| MC-E7 utility/sink integrity | SPLIT / FUTURE-WORK | Separate service-use persistence, participation displacement/crowding, and sink-state accounting. A sink is a token transition; utility is an actor-service relation. |

MC-V overlaps are dependencies only if made explicit: `A↔S` events/schedules;
`S↔D` recipients and compounding; `S↔T` funding; `T↔L` executable reserve
value; `D↔U` useful output and cohorts; and `F↔all` typed dynamics. They are
unsound as duplicated peer measurements.

Category repairs:

1. Supply states are non-exclusive unless separated into existence, control,
   availability, provenance, event, and derived-view axes.
2. Token destruction and operating expenditure require different terms.
3. “Stable” is an asset label, not a guaranteed liquidity state.
4. “Liquidable” is an execution function, not a stock attribute.
5. Quality and extraction need typed service/output objects and boundaries.
6. Feedback is a higher-order relation graph, not a peer stock/result.

The minimum repaired report is a record, not an arithmetic vector:

  }

Every report keeps its own time basis, units, evidence posture, predicate
provenance, and uncertainty. Non-compensation remains structural.

Minimal definition handoff:

- Treasury is a declared institutional/control boundary containing positions
  and obligations, not reserve value or spendable capacity.
- Reserve position records asset, quantity, denomination, controller/custodian,
  access, encumbrance, and obligation class.
- Spendable capacity is a function of reserves, size, route, time, and scenario.
- Epoch must split into typed clocks with an explicit crosswalk.
- Emission must split mint issuance, treasury disbursement, unlock, and token
  removal.
- Runway is the greatest interval in which opening spendable capacity plus
  executable inflows cover cumulative obligations due under a scenario.
- Token burn is destruction; operating outflow is transfer/expenditure.

Questions for Ostrom and Popper:

2. Who defines entity, cohort, service-use, and displacement boundaries and
   bears externalities outside them?
3. What closed-world completeness witness makes omitted units falsifiable?
4. What falsifies service utility rather than only failure to isolate it, and
   what prevents `indeterminate` from becoming FAIL?

## Cross-Review Final Position — Popper / Non-Vacuity

Evidence-status discipline:

- `observed_fail`: object, comparison, horizon, boundary, and predicate were
  frozen; adequate observations crossed the terminal.
- `observed_within_declared_bound`: one predicate held in one declared scenario;
  it is not a safety result.
- `indeterminate`: observations exist, but completeness, identification,
  measurement, or uncertainty prevents deciding the predicate.
- `unrunnable`: a required object, surface, initial state, comparison, or input
  is absent.
- `not_applicable`: evidence establishes that the prerequisite institution or
  obligation does not exist in the declared scenario. Corpus absence alone is
  insufficient.

## Group Convergence And Remaining Tension

The group converges on:

- no local monetary mechanism, experiment, health model, or policy witness;
- external analogues and component precedents rather than integrated ownership;
- MC-E1's accounting objective retained only with ontology repair;
- MC-E2, MC-E3, MC-E4, and MC-E7 split;
- MC-E5's method retained but its local instance unrunnable/future-work;
- generic MC-E6 killed and replaced by typed institutional loops;
- `burn` split into token destruction and operating outflow;
- independent per-object and per-edge evidence statuses;
- non-compensatory, explicitly incomplete reporting; and
- no metric-to-reward, reputation, governance, or authority path.

The surviving tension is between fixture form and institutional grounding.
Reproducible numbers are insufficient: every future fixture also needs a
legitimate actor/controller/obligation/service boundary and definitionally sound
objects. The current repo lacks the closed-world supply witness, local
obligations, market institution, service-use definition, and non-arbitrary
failure parameters needed for that triad.
