## Revision Posture And Ownership Discipline

The bounded corpus identifies a plausible coordination gap and a candidate
local research coordinator. It did not perform an exhaustive ownership sweep
and does not establish an exclusive owner for an integrated ResonantOS
monetary-circulation problem.

Three evidence relations remain distinct:

- **Method precedent:** an accounting, scenario, execution, comparison, or
  reporting form exists.
- **External precedent or analogue:** a formal model, observation,
  specification, or incident supplies variables or a failure shape inside its
  own institution.
- **Local witness:** a frozen ResonantOS object, initial state, comparison,
  observation, and predicate exist.

The corpus supplies methods and external precedents. It supplies no local
monetary-circulation witness. Every surviving local MC-E instance is currently
`unrunnable` ([skeptic convergence](robot-talks.md#group-convergence-and-remaining-tension)).

The official ResonantDAO whitepaper remains the only official primary source.
External evidence remains related precedent; admission here does not convert
its assumptions into `$RES` facts.

## Reconciled Research Dimensions

### Supply And Accounting

A flat list containing minted, treasury-held, locked, vested, claimable,
circulating, rewarded, transferred, and burned units is unsound. These labels
mix existence, control, availability, events, provenance, and derived views.

```text
unit {
  existence_status
  controller
  availability_status
  denomination
}
events[]
derived_views[]
```

`circulating` is derived. Mint issuance, treasury transfer, unlock, fee
transfer, reward disbursement, and `token_burn` are typed events. EIP-1559 and
UNI support transition separation, not a `$RES` taxonomy or initial state.

**Local status:** `unrunnable` until an authoritative opening register, unit
domain, initial state, event vocabulary, typed clocks, provenance, and
closed-world completeness witness exist.

### Schedules And Recurring Obligations

No one “release schedule” object survives. Split mint issuance, treasury
disbursement, unlock/vesting, activity-conditioned reward, token removal,
recipient/allocation, compounding, and post-subsidy obligation funding.

A schedule result cannot travel without reward basis, recipient process,
compounding/reinvestment, participation/entry/exit, and horizon. Filecoin,
Fanti, Budish, and EIP-1559 concern different objects, not votes for or against
one curve. TF-12 remains `AWAITS` crossover `T*`; TF-14 remains `AWAITS` a
divergence predicate and `C`. All bounds remain sourced, calibrated, or scenario
parameters—never safe limits.

**Local status:** every schedule branch is `unrunnable`.

### Holdings, Access, And Newcomer Acquisition

Split holdings distribution over a declared actor map, access distribution over
a functional-access definition, newcomer acquisition route/time/cost, cohort
membership/entry epoch, and redistribution/exit. Fanti, Kondor, UNI, and
Filecoin supply distinct precedents; none owns a local actor map, access
predicate, or threshold.

“Reward per useful unit” is removed until a service/output unit exists. No
distribution output becomes merit, contribution credit, reputation, governance
weight, or authority.

**Local status:** all distribution branches are `unrunnable`.

### Treasury, Solvency, Obligation Liquidity, And Runway

Split the treasury/runway aggregate into institutional control boundary,
reserve positions, balance-sheet solvency, obligation-matched spendable
capacity, obligation liquidity/due-time coverage, and conditional runway.

`token_burn` is irreversible unit destruction. `operating_outflow` is transfer
or expenditure to meet an obligation. They are not interchangeable.

`liquidable` is not a stock attribute. Use:

```text
spendable_capacity(
  reserve_positions,
  target_denomination,
  size,
  route_set,
  time,
  market_state,
  scenario
)
```

Runway is the greatest declared interval over which opening spendable capacity
plus executable inflows cover cumulative obligations due. A positive amount
does not imply adequate duration. TF-13 survives as a scenario-schema form; it
is not a local observation and does not prove that native assets have zero
executable value.

**Local status:** all treasury/runway objects are `unrunnable`.

### Market Execution And Exit Capacity

Retain route replay as a method:

```text
exit_capacity(
  source_asset,
  target_asset_or_numeraire,
  order_size,
  route_set,
  market_state,
  provider_set,
  execution_horizon,
  scenario
)
```

Distinguish market sale, redemption, auction liquidation, and treasury
liquidation. The admitted sources show that TVL/volume can diverge from
executable depth; they do not estimate how commonly or rapidly this occurs in
runs. “The market” is not an actor: providers, withdrawal rights, inclusion,
routing, oracle dependencies, and target numéraire must be named.

**Method status:** retained.  
**Local status:** `FUTURE-WORK` and `unrunnable`; no `$RES` market object exists.

### Service Use, Persistence, Displacement, And Sinks

Split qualifying service-use event, post-intervention persistence,
subsidy/appreciation/yield/buyback comparison arms, system-wide displacement,
extraction boundary, and sink-state accounting.

A sink is a unit availability/state transition. Utility is an actor-service
relation. They cannot share one “integrity” value. The behavioral sources own
bounded effects, not the sign of repeated Resonant contribution incentives.
Failure to isolate subsidy/speculation is `indeterminate`, not evidence that
utility is absent.

**Local status:** all service-use, persistence, displacement, and sink branches
are `unrunnable`.
