## Ownership Distinctions

1. **Method precedent:** an accounting, comparison, stress, execution, or
   reporting form exists.
2. **External precedent or analogue:** a source supplies variables,
   observations, or a failure shape inside its own institution.
3. **Local witness:** a frozen ResonantOS object, initial state, comparison,
   observation, and predicate exist.

This dispatch found the first two and no third. The tower is at most a candidate
local coordinator identified by a bounded, non-exhaustive sweep; it is not the

## Reconciled Object Families

### Unit And Accounting Objects

```text
unit {
  unit_domain
  denomination
  existence_status
  controller
  availability_status
  provenance
}
```

Mint issuance, treasury transfer, unlock, fee transfer, reward disbursement,
and `token_burn` are events. `circulating` is a derived view, not one exclusive
state. A local report requires an authoritative opening register, frozen event
vocabulary, state relationships, ingress/egress provenance, and a closed-world
completeness witness. Missing provenance is `indeterminate` or `unrunnable`,
not observed failure ([supply repair](synthesis-revised.md#supply-and-accounting)).

### Typed Clocks

`epoch` must split into accounting period, emission period, vesting interval,
obligation due time, execution horizon, and observation window, with a crosswalk.
This is a candidate ER-D25 handoff, not a promoted redefinition.

### Schedules And Recurring Obligations

Split mint issuance, treasury disbursement, unlock/vesting,
activity-conditioned reward, token removal, allocation/recipient selection,
compounding, and post-subsidy obligation funding. A result cannot travel unless
reward basis, recipient process, compounding, participation assumptions, and
horizon match ([Myerson return](research.md#agent-1--myerson-roger)).

### Holdings, Access, And Newcomer Acquisition

Holdings distribution, access distribution, acquisition route/time/cost,
actor resolution, cohort membership, entry epoch, redistribution, and exit are
different objects. “Reward per useful unit” remains undefined until a service
unit exists. No balance or access report becomes credit, merit, reputation,
governance, or authority.

### Treasury, Solvency, Obligation Liquidity, And Runway

Treasury is an institutional/control boundary containing positions and
obligations. DAO, foundation, manager, custodian, legal access, and off-chain
controller remain distinct. Solvency, spendable capacity, due-time liquidity,
and runway are separate reports ([Taleb return](research.md#agent-2--taleb-nassim-nicholas)).

`token_burn` is irreversible destruction. `operating_outflow` is transfer or
expenditure to satisfy an obligation. They are disjoint.

```text
spendable_capacity(
  reserve_positions,
  target_denomination,
  size,
  route_set,
  time,
  market_state,
  scenario
)
```

Runway is the greatest declared interval over which opening spendable capacity
plus executable inflows cover cumulative obligations due. A positive numerator
does not establish adequate duration.

### Market Execution And Exit Capacity

```text
exit_capacity(
  source_asset,
  target_asset_or_numeraire,
  order_size,
  route_set,
  market_state,
  provider_set,
  execution_horizon,
  scenario
)
```

Market sale, redemption, auction liquidation, and treasury liquidation remain
distinct. Providers, withdrawal rights, inclusion, routing, oracle dependencies,
and numéraire must be explicit. No source-backed `$RES` market object is
admitted in this dispatch
([Fritz return](research.md#agent-3--fritz-tobias)).

### Service Use, Persistence, Displacement, Extraction, And Sinks

Qualifying service use, persistence, subsidy/appreciation/yield/buyback arms,
system displacement, extraction boundary, and sink-state accounting are
separate. A sink is a unit transition; utility is an actor-service relation.
When the required isolation comparison is absent, the local handoff is
`unrunnable`. When an applicable comparison has been observed but cannot
identify service use separately from subsidy, appreciation, yield, buyback
expectation, or displacement, its report is `indeterminate`. Neither status is
evidence that utility is absent
([Bowles return](research.md#agent-4--bowles-samuel)).

### Typed Relation Graph

Feedback is a relation graph, not a seventh peer measure. Every edge requires:

  source_object,
  target_object,
  sign,
  delay,
  institution,
  evidence,
  intervention,
  counterfactual,
  failure_predicate
}
```

Compounding, treasury sale, collateral delay, `market_impact_exit_feedback`,
range crossing, price attention, reward displacement, and open-loop
affordability branches are all individually `unrunnable`. Generic aggregate
MC-E6 is killed; no generic feedback verdict survives.

```

There is no top-level PASS, FAIL, evidence status, score, rank, or health
verdict. Unknown objects and relations remain residue. Dependencies do not
duplicate or compensate results
([MC-R contract](synthesis-revised.md#explicitly-incomplete-non-compensatory-mc-r)).

Evidence statuses:

- `observed_fail`: adequate observations crossed one frozen predicate;
- `observed_within_declared_bound`: one predicate held in one scenario—not
  safety, validation, or readiness;
- `indeterminate`: observations exist but cannot adjudicate the predicate;
- `unrunnable`: a required object, surface, state, comparison, or input is
  absent; and
- `not_applicable`: evidence establishes that a prerequisite institution or
  obligation does not exist; corpus silence is insufficient.

`indeterminate` and `unrunnable` never mean neutral evidence or zero risk. No
current local report qualifies as `observed_fail` or
`observed_within_declared_bound`.
