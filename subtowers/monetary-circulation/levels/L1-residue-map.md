# L1 Residue Map

Status: open; refined after accepted bounded research
Promotion scope: local-research-only

| ID | Residue | Collapse or next-check question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MC-R1 | Supply-state identity | Which states are mutually exclusive, which are nested, and how is double counting prevented? |
| MC-R2 | Initial conditions | Which treasury, circulating, locked, or reserved quantities are source-backed rather than assumed? |
| MC-R3 | Schedule basis | Is the release schedule source-given, fixture-declared, or merely a scenario? |
| MC-R4 | Epoch choice | How does changing the accounting period change affordability or failure detection? |
| MC-R5 | Finite versus long horizon | When does a short-horizon advantage reverse into concentration, depletion, or instability? |
| MC-R6 | Distribution witness | Which concentration and access measures expose harm without becoming a person score or authority surface? |
| MC-R7 | Treasury value | What portion of treasury value is actually spendable under stress rather than marked at an endogenous token price? |
| MC-R8 | Reserve correlation | Which reserves fail together under the shocks the treasury must survive? |
| MC-R9 | Obligations and burn | Which recurring or contingent obligations define runway, and over what period? |
| MC-R10 | Liquidity source | Who supplies liquidity, why do they remain, and what happens when their incentive changes? |
| MC-R11 | Exit capacity | What amount can exit within declared price-impact and time-to-cash bounds during ordinary and stressed demand? |
| MC-R12 | Manipulation | Which market, oracle, pool, routing, or governance surfaces can be manipulated? |
| MC-R13 | Utility | Which observed uses persist without subsidy, yield, appreciation, or buyback expectation? |
| MC-R14 | Sink integrity | Does a proposed sink represent real use, forced demand, circular subsidy, or value destruction? |
| MC-R15 | Reflexivity | Which price, treasury, emission, participation, and liquidity edges form reinforcing loops? |
| MC-R16 | Governor | What variable does a proposed governor observe, what action can it take, and what bounded failure condition must it hold? |
| MC-R17 | Internal/external boundary | Which conclusions hold only before external liquidity, and which new failure surfaces appear after it? |
| MC-R18 | Phase 2 gate | What evidence is required before any financial mechanism becomes designable rather than merely researchable? |
| MC-R19 | Aggregate-score collapse | Can every terminal failure remain visible without a weighted health score masking it? |
| MC-R20 | Experiment readiness | Which candidate handoff has grounded terms, frozen scenarios, ethical limits, and a runnable evidence schema? |

Related prior art may revise these rows but cannot close empirical residue by
itself. Measured claims require a separate evidence-harness or experiment route.
