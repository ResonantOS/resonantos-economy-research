- a treasury witness values native assets at an unstressed endogenous price;
- liquidity is inferred from total supply, price, or headline pool value alone;
- numeric thresholds are imported without source posture or calibration;
- a utility claim depends only on yield, subsidy, appreciation, or circular
  buyback expectation;
- a feedback governor has no bounded observed variable or failure criterion;
- a monetary-health score hides a terminal failure dimension; or
- the next step would select policy, implementation, market deployment, or
  financial launch.
